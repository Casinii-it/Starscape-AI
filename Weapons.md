Weapons

Weapons are items used in ground combat to damage other players and ships inside space stations.

## Weapon Subcategories

### Rifles

Rifles are automatic weapons that fire by holding LMB. Firing for an extended period will overheat the weapon; overheated rifles can't fire for a few seconds.

## Grenades

Grenades are bought from NPC marketplaces. Holding LMB will ready the grenade. After letting go, the grenade will be thrown. There are three types of grenades: Explosive Grenades, Smoke Grenades, and Poison Grenades.

### Explosive Grenades

Explosive Grenades explode shortly after hitting the ground, damaging anyone near them. Explosive grenades can also damage ships

## Applications

**- Duels**: while not altogether practical, these are a fun use of weapons that you can do at nearly any time. Grab one, challenge to duel, and start blasting; maybe even bet something if you're confident enough.

**- Dock** **Shredding**: When a player is inside a docking bay within their ship, they are immune to damage from other ships, but not from weapons. In a squad, if you have player ships that prevent an enemy ship from docking or leaving the station, other members can get out of their ships and begin firing at them.

**- Last Stand:** Occasionally, you will be in a scenario where you cannot fight in ship combat against an enemy, and instead want to beat them on a more even playing field, so going to a station, unloading items, and equipping weapons and armor can be a last resort.

**- Raids:** A popular pirating strategy is first checking if a player is inside a station, then rushing in with quick ships and killing any player inside.
