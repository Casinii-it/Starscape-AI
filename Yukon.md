Yukon

*"The Yukon is a giant mining platform which can efficiently strip mine entire asteroid fields."*

The Yukon is a miner-class ship of the Everest ship line. It is best suited as an expensive but efficient miner, boasting the highest mining rate in the game.

## Advantages

The Yukon has more health and more rig slots than the lighter ships in its line, the Ozark and Kodiak. It also has more PDT slots, as well as some small-turret slots on its underside, allowing for better self-defense against weak enemies.

The addition of two cargo slots allows it to store more ore and stay in wild for longer.

The Yukon has a +125% mining speed bonus, by far the highest mining rate buff of any mining ship.

## Disadvantages

As a heavy destroyer-miner, it has very poor mobility.

Although it can equip combat turrets, it's not a combat ship and has fewer hitpoints than most destroyers, non-ideal turret placement and a 20% damage penalty on all turrets. Oftentimes, a Yukon by its lonesome is an easy target for just one or two pirates. This issue is generally negated by situational awareness and avoiding players at all costs.

It is very expensive and requires more resources to craft than other Destroyers.

## Trivia

* The current Yukon is modeled by Drezzlez.

* Due to its mining turret limitations, pilots can equip 4 combat turrets on the Yukon without sacrificing any mining capability.
