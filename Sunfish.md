Sunfish

*"Lycentian destroyer"*



The Sunfish is a cruiser-type ship of the Megalodon ship line and is only playable by Developers . It is the most well-known cruiser, with the most filled out stats. Also seen in Foralkan NPC fleets.

## Advantages

* Its turret agility is buffed by 25% allowing the user to have an advantage in tracking.
* As a cruiser, The Sunfish almost always has a complement of fighters and corvettes to support it

## Disadvantages

* Like all cruisers, it is very slow. This means it can easily be kited without the use of a light burner.
* Due to the Foralkan philosophy of side-heavy turret placement, the Sunfish's turret placement suffers heavily in a horizontal manner. Vertically, however, it can fire almost every turret in a 360 degree angle.
* As most of its health is in its hull, kinetic weapons are quite effective against it.

## Trivia

* There are two large turrets on the Sunfish
* This is the only flyable cruiser in the main game, obtained by Developers
* The legacy Foralkan Cruiser model was called 'Hammerhead'
* This crusier abandons the Foralkan ship path of pure firepower, instead taking on turret agility like the Lycentians.
