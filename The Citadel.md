The Citadel

![](./File:Roblox_9_8_2020_3_21_33_PM_(2).png)

The Citadel station.

*'The Citadel' is the name of the star system at the center of the galaxy and also the name of planet 2 in that system. 'Citadel' is the name of the station in the warp hub of the Citadel planet.*

The Citadel is a military station owned by CoreSec located in the central region of the galaxy, serving as the starting point for new players and the default spawn point. The station has 3 floors: the upper floor, the lower floor, and the docking bays, connected by elevators.

**- The upper floor** contains CoreSec's cloning bay, a special marketplace module, a medical facility, an observatory, a transfer terminal, and the Syndicate's representative.

**- The lower floor** has a refinery, a manufacturing facility, a repair bay, a CoreSec field office, and the Mining Guild's embassy.

**- The docking bays** have 4 docking bay modules and the embassies of the remaining factions.

## Embassies

Ambassador NPCs can receive standing items from players and can reset their negative faction standing for credits; for the warring factions, their embassies also have a recruiter NPC, allowing players to enlist, resign, and apply for promotions in their militaries.

During one of the tutorial missions, *Welcome to the Galaxy*, the ambassadors give players dialogue detailing the background and lore of their factions.

## System Location

The Citadel system is in the central region of the Core. It is connected to 6 other core star systems:

* Bastion - 15.2 light-years away
* Aegis - 17.2 light-years away
* Shield - 17.8 light-years away
* Concord - 19.3 light-years away
* Guard - 24.1 light-years away
* Triage - 12.0 light-years away

The Citadel is in a strategic position due to its proximity to the capital systems of every other major faction.

* Lycentia - 8 jumps or 1 jump and 2 relay jumps.
* Hippa - 8 jumps or 1 jump and 2 relay jumps.
* Vanos - 10 jumps or 1 jump and 2 relay jumps.
* Harbor - 5 jumps or 1 jump and 1 relay jump.
* Quarry - 19 jumps or 1 jump and 4 relay jumps.
* Syndica - 23 jumps or 14 jumps and 3 relay jumps.

## Points of Interest

Beyond the station, there are 2 asteroid fields, one in Bantam and one in the Citadel's ring, having only inferior korrelite and reknite. These are common places to find new Strati chiseling away at asteroids, doing the *Getting Started* and *Intro to Manufacturing* tutorial missions.
