Mackerel

*"The Mackerel is a Foralkan interceptor."*

The Mackerel is a T1 interceptor-class ship of the Megalodon ship line. It is best suited as a agile and heavy-hitting interceptor.

## Advantages

The Mackerel is the ship with the highest agility in the game, despite being a T1 interceptor, which means it is better equipped against other starships than the Candiru, while also having the second highest DPS among the interceptors. Mackerel's average stats for its class in everything except hull and shield allow it to be used as a standalone ship, even while having an upgraded form.

## Disadvantages

The Mackerel is still a T1 interceptor and, therefore, most stats are lower than the Candiru outside of its agility niche. Additionally, low hull and shield makes it vulnerable to turrets with good tracking, preventing it from taking on warships.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
