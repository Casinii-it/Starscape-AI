Manufacturing

![](./File:ManufacturingStationModule.png)

Manufacturing module.

Crafting is a system in Starscape that allows you to create and upgrade certain items using materials you've collected throughout the universe.

Another word of importance is that it will only be made and completed at the Manufacturing bay you originally made it. That means, yes, you have to go back all the way to that system, find the station, and go into the menu to collect it.

### Modules

* You can craft T1, T2, and T3 modules.
* You cannot craft Exotic modules.
* You cannot craft Lightburners.
* Modules take 30 seconds to craft.

### Turrets

* You can craft T1, T2, T3, Hybrid, and Dread turrets.
* You cannot craft Ancient turrets.
* Small turrets take 30 seconds to craft, Medium turrets 1 minute and Mining turrets - 10 seconds.

### Deployables

* You can craft Spacemines and Probes (Planet, System, and Interstellar variants).
* Planet and System probes have tiers of T1-T2 while Interstellar probes are T2-T3
* You cannot craft Fireworks.
* You can craft Warp Disruptors of T1-T3
* Deployables take 10 seconds to craft.

### Weapons

* You can craft Pistols and Rifles.
* Weapons take 10 seconds to craft.

### Armor

* You can craft Helmets, Torsos and Legs.
* Armor takes 10 seconds to craft.

### Components

* Out of all components, you can only craft a Heat Sink.
* Components take 10 seconds to craft.

## Trivia

* Leaving the game while crafting something that takes long will still craft and will continue crafting untill the craft is done.
* As of the "Getting Around" Update there is only one craftable component in the game - that being the Heat Sink.
