The **Spectator** is a ship used exclusively by Starscape's Admins, mainly for taking thumbnail photos and capturing in-game footage. Not much is known to the public about this ship, as it has made very few appearances to players in-game. The ship is a large, spheroid with a single engine. The Spectator boasts no weapons. Due to the usage and stats of the ship, it is not intended to be used during normal gameplay.

## Advantages

The Spectator has the second highest health stats of all ships in the game, with 10,000 hull points, 10,000 hull regeneration, 10,000 shield points, and 10,000 shield regeneration. The Spectator is also the 2nd fastest and most agile ship in the game, having a maximum speed of 500 m/s, 500 acceleration speed, 200 agility points, and a warp speed of 10 AU/s. Its stats are second only to the Spectator-X.

## Disadvantages

It has no armament. That's it. You can't kill this thing, so it honestly doesn't matter.
