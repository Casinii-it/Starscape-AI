Chevron

*"The Chevron is a workhorse warship that makes up for its lack in agility with strength and stubbornness."*

The Chevron is a corvette-class ship of the Legionis ship line. It is best suited as a sturdy fleet ship.

## Advantages

Unlike any warship of its line, it has relatively average agility and speed, and has excellent turret placement, giving it full broadside firepower and a solid niche in 1v1 situations against larger ships.

For more casual players, the Chevron is a corvette with defenses superior to some frigates, giving a jumpstart after coming from the more fragile starfighters.

## Disadvantages

The Chevron has the standard Legionis warship weaknesses: kinetic weapons and kiting; it also relies too much on hull repairers due to having a single subsystem slot.

## Trivia

- The current Chevron is modeled by ii\_Cove and Axaraz.

- The Chevron was formerly named the Dolphin due to the fin on its Pre-Next Gen model.
