Hybrid Ships

Hybrid Ships are sidegrades to the fighters and interceptors of neutral ship lines, usually sacrificing defense for improved mobility and DPS.

## Crafting

Hybrid ships require the advanced version of the fighter (i.e. you need a Luminar to craft a Hybrid Luminar), 100 power cells, a Drone Command Core, 2 Vexnium, and the respective Hybrid blueprint. Each Hybrid Fighter takes 1 hour to craft. To obtain Hybrid Ship Blueprints, destroy Elite or Command Drone ships, and Drone Battleships. The exact drop rate is unknown, but it is very low.

## Trivia

* Before the *Next Gen* update, Hybrid ships used to be called as X-Ships.
* X-Ships used to be much harder to craft as they required 2 BPs to be crafted. These blueprints are labelled with “1/2” and “2/2”, where you would need to get both of them. The blueprint’s rarity made them expensive, and in turn made the crafted ship very pricey to purchase. The two blueprints both had a large price difference due to the possible drop rate difference back then.
* X-Ships used to have a dark color scheme with glowing yellow stripes, but this was changed to a more lighter color but with the same glowing stripes (unless the skin applied changes it) to make it easier to be seen and visible.
