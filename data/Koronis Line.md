Koronis Line

The Koronis line, created by the Mining Guild, features miner-hauler hybrids that offset their lower mining rate with cargo slots to store large amounts of loot, ores and probes.

### Details

The cargo slots of these miners allow players to explore dozens of systems without running out of inventory space, and if they have a built player structure or know the location of an available one, they can haul station fuel around to power modules to store items and refine ores, further increasing their longevity on wild space expeditions.

## Trivia

* The Koronis Line vessels are named after asteroids of the Asteroid Belt, located between the orbits of Mars and Jupiter.
* Prior to the *Getting Around* update, their blueprints were obtained with the Trade Union.
  + This is why the warships of the Koronis and Nebula lines have similar designs, such as the rectangular central hull with a window-like detail at the front.
