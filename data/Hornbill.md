Hornbill

*"The Hornbill is an upgraded variant of the Kavani Toucan."*

The Hornbill is a T2 fighter-class ship of the Mandator ship line. It is best suited as a tanky shield-focused supportive fighter.

## Advantages

* Three subsystem slots grant the Hornbill great support capacity, and lets it run versatile setups.
* The Hornbill is fast and agile for a fighter, and has a very fast warp speed that's comparable to most interceptors.
* It has high balanced hit-points for a fighter, and decent shield regeneration.
* On top of strong overall defensive stats, it also boasts the potential for rapid energy regeneration.

## Disadvantages

* While tanky for a fighter, its wings occupy most of its hitbox and render it easy to hit.
* Despite carrying a weapon rig, the Hornbill's damage is under-powered for three hardpoints, and suffers most against high deflection/armor ratings.
* As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
