Knifejaw

*"The Knifejaw corvette is a solid and versatile warship. The Foralkan Armada makes extensive use of it in small, agile attack fleets."*

The Knifejaw is a corvette-class ship of the Megalodon ship line. It is best suited as a brawling ship due to its tank and damage.

## Advantages

The Knifejaw has high HP and damage due to its role bonuses, and its turret placement can shoot 360 degrees vertically, making it a good brawling ship that can circle around its target. However, It has low shields. As a Foralkan ship, any weaponry equipped on it will be augmented by its bonus. This is also the highest damaging corvette according to raw damage dealt without any modules.

## Disadvantages

The Knifejaw has a hull focus, restricting its methods of retreating into station for players with less confidence. In addition its turret placement is strange for inexperienced players to pick up.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

## Trivia

- The current Knifejaw is modeled by ii\_Cove.
