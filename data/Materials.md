Materials

Resources are a category of items that serve as the basis for all manufacturing recipes in Starscape, obtained by mining in planetary asteroid fields, anomalies, and aberrations, looting secure containers in X-structures, and NPC ship and structure wrecks.

Resources are found in two forms: ores, which have no other use besides being refined, and minerals/chemicals, which are used for manufacturing.

## Types of Resources

The game currently has 9 resources total, divided into 2 subcategories: minerals and chemicals.

### Minerals

**Basic:** korrelite, reknite, and gellium. These are used in large quantities for almost everything in the game and are practically always available, found in lower quantities in PvP-disabled systems and in higher quantities in PvP-enabled systems.

**Advanced:** axnit, narcor (blucor), and red narcor (redcor). These are much more difficult to acquire in bulk, with just a few asteroids in any given region, more commonly found in wild space, rare anomalies, and as loot from stronger NPC enemies. Advanced ores are mostly used to upgrade ship modifications and equipment, and to craft bigger and stronger ships.

**Guarded:** vexnium. Vexnium is only found in specific anomalous regions, requiring miner turrets to be mined, but doing so will activate previously dormant enemies. It is used to make the strongest available player ships.

### Chemicals

**Water and heavy water.** Chemicals are found in comets and ice deposits, with drastically reduced yields without ice miners. They are essential in the recipe of specialized miner turrets and fuel for player structures.
