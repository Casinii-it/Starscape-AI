Vexnium Fields

![](./File:VX_anomaly_field_with_ozark.png)

An Ozark in an activated VX anomaly

**Vexnium fields**, found in-game as VX anomalies and V aberrations, are the only source of vexnium, a rare and expensive material that **can only be mined with miner turrets** used in the construction of destroyers and hybrid starships. Vexnium fields can only be found in wild systems.

They contain 1-4 vexnium asteroids, and 2-5 hibernating **???** NPCs (unoffically referred to as crystalline guardians) that guard the vexnium. The entire asteroid field is filled with a black nebula and cosmetic black and blue-spiked asteroids. Lightning flashes occasionally, inflicting up to 500 Damage.

## Mechanic

The field's guardians are dormant until a player mines a single vexnium ore. They will transform in shape and move like ships, firing lightning-like attacks that deal around 300 splash damage per shot.

## Mining Strategies

### Avoiding the Damage

You can mine vexnium ore while staying out of the guardians' detection range (2000 meters). However, it can be time-consuming if the ore is in the middle of the cloud, because the guardians will be closer to it more often.

### Taking the Damage

This strategy consists on depleting the vexnium asteroids as quickly as possible while withstanding guardian attacks and warping out or using a lightburner once you're done. Only works with destroyer miners (and maybe the Vesta), as they have the most defense and mining rate. Aways carry a hull repairer and a set of strip miners when doing this.

### Non-Miner Ships

If you're not piloting a miner ship, it's better to use a precision miner, so you don't also need a tractor turret.
