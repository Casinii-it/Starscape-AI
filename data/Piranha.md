Piranha

*"The Piranha is a Foralkan fighter based on the Sabre chassis. It is more nimble than the Sabre, though also cannot take quite as much of a beating during a fight."*

The Piranha is a T1 fighter-class ship of the Megalodon ship line. It is best suited as an assault fighter, taking down ships in quick bursts of damage.

## Advantages

* The Piranha has impressive speed and agility for an entry-level fighter, and a small overall profile, making it harder to hit.
* It deals the highest damage per-projectile of all fighters and interceptors, making high deflection and armor a non-issue. On top of having two weapon module slots to further amplify its damage or prolong its volleys of fire.

## Disadvantages

* As a tier 1 fighter, it may not always perform up to par against more reliable, higher-tier fighters and interceptors.
* It lacks any means of self-preservation and has weak shields and hull. Making it easy to kill for PDT's.
* As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
