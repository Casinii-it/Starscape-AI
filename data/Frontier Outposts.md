Frontier Outposts

## Syndicate Frontier Outpost

![FO anomaly](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starscape-roblox/images/b/bf/FO_anomaly.PNG/revision/latest?cb=20200901142228 "FO anomaly")


A Syndicate frontier outpost station.



Frontier outposts are Syndicate stations with a shop terminal and a gemstone spice platform found only in FO anomalies. Alongside the station, there is a regular korrelite asteroid field and Syndicate fighters patrolling the area.

## Outpost Modules

### Docking Bay

Outpost docking bays are octagonal in shape and have 3 entrances/exits. The door providing access to the rest of the station is elevated, with 2 access ramps. Interacting with the dockyard terminal does not open a menu; it only generates the ship the player entered the outpost with. These docking bays have coilguns and railguns instead of cannons and beams.

### Main Room

![](./File:Frontier_Outpost_Interior.png)

The interior of a Frontier Outpost, with the market terminal, Restock, and Field Depletion timers.

Inside the main room, there's a walkway around a huge asteroid, and right in front of the docking bay doors is the shop terminal, with two timers next to it.

The shop terminal gives players a selection of items to purchase with credits. It has the same UI and item pool as Syndicate freighters.

The Restock Timer indicates when the shop's inventory is refreshed (24-hour cycle), and the Field Depletion Timer indicates how long until the anomaly expires (7 days after being found by a player). The Restock Timer format is HH: MM, while the Field Depletion Timer format is D: HH: MM.

### Dockyard

Dockyard modules at outposts do not sell supplies; instead, from their cargo dock, the FO sells gemstone spice and buys spice from players. The color of the spice sold and purchasing values depend on the system's location relative to the colored spice regions.

Frontier outposts can be used in conjunction with wormholes to haul large quantities of spice across the galaxy, resulting in considerable profits for the player. See WH/FO Hauling for more information.

### Shipyards

Shipyards are empty rooms from the inside, but from the outside, they are attached to an enclosure surrounding an unmarked Jovian transport ship.

### Alternating Modules

**- Reactors** contain long vertical reactor cores, which presumably power their outpost. It has an enclosed walkway around the reactor, with windows facing both in and out.

**- Drills** contain a large drill. Beneath the station, it connects to an asteroid similar to the one in the main room, which is held in place by a clamp.

## Trivia

* Frontier outposts were added on 5/17/2020 in the Scanning Probe Update, along with the Solar System map and Tier 3 Monuments.
* Throughout Starscape's history, the shop in FOs accidentally offered unreleased and unobtainable items for short periods of time.
  + Ice miners used to be purchasable from FOs before they were officially added to the game.
  + Prospector turrets were also sold, but this item never made it into the game officially. The few sold by FOs remain the only ones in existence.
  + Snowballs (the inventory item, not the ones obtained via snow piles) used to be sold by FOs as well. Throwing it uses it up, but some are still in circulation.
* FOs were the only way to get tier IV medium turrets before being replaced by exotic turrets.
* FOs are often used in EVA glitches because their thin walls make it easy to glitch through.
* FOs were once glitched and able to spawn in secure systems. However, when that happened, the shop terminal and dockyard didn't work, and neither timer went down.
