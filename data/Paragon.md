Paragon

*"An advanced upgraded variant of the Falcon, the Paragon is one of the fastest and most agile ships in the galaxy."*

The Paragon is an interceptor-class ship of the Defiance ship line. It is best suited as a fast scouting ship, and is the upgraded version of the Falcon.

## Advantages

The Paragon is one of the most maneuverable ships in the game, only second to the Hybrid Paragon. This gives it unrivaled scouting abilities, and lets it escape from almost any situation. Its warp speed and charge time can be augmented to be one of the best in the game, second only to the Mako. It is preferred by player pirates for scouting ahead and pinning down ships for this reason.

## Disadvantages

Compared to the other advanced starships in the game, the Paragon has some of the weakest durability and DPS stats. This means that while the Paragon is excellent at going ahead of the fleet to conduct quick operations, it cannot complete more challenging tasks that other starships could.
