Ethereal Pirates

## Overview

**Ethereal Pirates** were introduced on October 30th, 2020 for the 2020 Halloween event. Ethereal pirates were NPC pirate ships that had a glowing appearance. When killed these ships dropped a one-time use ship skin applicable to a specific ship.

## Location

Ethereal ships spawned in unsecure or wild space pirate fleets. At the time, fleets and patrols were uncommon to encounter and took time to find. Only one ethereal pirate could spawn per fleet.

## Trivia

* Ethereal pirate ships were ten times more durable than normal pirate ships.
* The 2020 Halloween Event ended on November 6th, 2020, making this a limited-quantity skin.
* Ethereal corvettes and frigates were considered mini-bosses.
* An Ethereal Phalanx would have 17400 total health, more than a Crystalline Guardian.
