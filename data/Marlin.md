Marlin

*"The Marlin is a nimble vessel that is designed to mine efficiently but be capable of making a quick escape when necessary."*

The Marlin is an entry-level miner-class ship of the Marlin ship line. It is best suited as a cheap starter mining ship.

## Advantages

The Marlin is cheap and can be bought at most NPC marketplaces, making it the ship of choice alongside the Badger for new players. Compared to the Badger, it has a slower mining rate but is better at escaping enemies.

## Disadvantages

The Marlin is an entry-level ship, and thus will perform poorly compared to upgraded miners. It can escape pirates, but is extremely fragile, and lacks any combat abilities whatsoever.
