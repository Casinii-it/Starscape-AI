## Ion turret

## Ion Turrets (Small)

Ion turrets are like inverted coilguns. They do 0.65x damage do hull, and 1.25x damage to shield.

Because most ships are hull-based, these turrets are not popular; however, they would be quite effective against Righteous-line or Constellation-line ships.

 Ion turrets come in Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, and an Exotic variant. The Ion turret's only Exotic variant is Hybrid. Hybrid Ion turrets must be crafted from Drone Cores, Power Cells, a Tier 3 Ion turret, and a Hybrid Ion blueprint dropped by high-tier Drones and Drone Factories.

## Ion turret

## Ion-M Turrets (Medium)

Ion-M turrets are very similar to their S variant. They have greater firepower and lower maneuverability. **Medium turrets can only be equipped onto ships with Medium Turret Slots.**

Ion-M turrets can be Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, or an Exotic variant. The Ion-M's only Exotic variant is Hybrid. Hybrid Ion-M turrets must be crafted from Drone Cores, a Tier 3 Ion-M turret, and a Hybrid Ion-M blueprint dropped by high-tier Drones and Drone Factories.
