**???**, or crystalline guardians, are NPCs that spawn in VX anomalies and V deposits. Each vexnium field will have 2 to 5 of these within them. They have no shield and fire lightning bolt attacks at players.

When destroyed, crystalline guardians always drop a single crystal shard, a component item that can be used to craft siege fuel.

## Charateristics

### Behavior

Upon warping in a vexnium field, the guardians are dormant and resemble a large blue crystal. Once a piece of vexnium is mined, a loud screech is heard, indicating their activation. Any guardian in range will immediately fire at the player which mined the vexnium, regardless of other players in the area.

After 30 minutes of peace, the guardians deactivate returning to their dormant state. However, they can be reactivated by mining another vexnium ore.

### **Armament**

These NPCs use special weapons that fire high-speed, lightning-like attacks, with a maximum range of 5000 meters, depleting around 300 hitpoints per shot within a large AoE. It is also more effective against shielding (like ion turrets).

## Combat Strategies

### Miner Ships

First of all, not only you shouldn't be fighting **anything** in a non-combat vessel, you would have to sacrifice mining power to do so. However, **the Yukon, Phantom and Juno** are the only exceptions, as they have more turret placements than design restrictions for miner turrets.

The most viable strategy for these ships is to kill the guardians one by one with railguns, flying along the edges of the asteroid field so that some bolts completely miss you. Always carry a hull repairer or booster.

### Combat Ships

* **Corvettes -** To actually be able to mine the vexnium, a corvette sacrifices 1/4 of its DPS to equip a miner turret, and this, along with their lower amount of hitpoints, encourages the same strategy as miner ships.

* **Frigates and Destroyers -** These ships can more confidently face the guardians, allowing for standard PVE options, such as cannons, coilguns and blasters.
