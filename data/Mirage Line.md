Mirage Line

The Mirage line, built by The Syndicate, features warship miners with decent field stats, except for defense. Their engine rigs allow them to spend the least amount of time outside of warp, and their low signal range can prevent players from spotting these ships, especially with the use of lightburners.

## Trivia

* The Mirage line ships are named after ghostly apparitions, referencing the line's focus on remaining unseen.
