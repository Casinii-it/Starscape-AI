> - The Lycentian Federation's career tab description.

The Lycentian Federation, located on the northeastern side of the Core, based in the Catalyst sector and maintaining sovereignty over most of Sentinel, is one of the three warring factions in Starscape. This nation possesses the largest permanent territory when excluding CoreSec's Non-Aligned Territories and fights with the Kavani Mandate for control over Contact, and the Foralkan Star Empire, over Schism.

Lycentia's relay network serves as a crossroads for players traveling to the Syndicate and CZ Alpha, meaning that systems outside its wild space borders are heavily affected by piracy.

The slang term for this nation is "Brick" or "Brickers," due to the blocky build of their older warship models.

## History

The Lycentian Federation is the oldest and most well-established nation in the galaxy, but, in the period the game takes place, it is a nation in decline. In the last century, with the expansion of the Kavani, a previously non-threatening nation, and the Foralkan, a breakaway state from the Federation itself, the Lycentians lost their position of uncontested power and were pushed back from all sides, both territorially and ideologically.

Now, the Federation finds itself cornered in a power struggle with other superpowers, as well as with CoreSec, preventing it from ever definitively reclaiming all the territories lost in this war. This is reflected in the design of its ships: while the Mandator and Megalodon ships are obviously built for conquest, the Federal Starfleet is composed of defensive ships, designed to protect what it still has, hoping to outlast its rivals in this war of attrition.

It is reasonable to assume that, before the current war began, the Federation had control over more than half of the Outer Core, including Catalyst and Sentinel, the entire Contact sector, and therefore bordering the First Mandate, and the current Empyrean Highlands and Schism.
