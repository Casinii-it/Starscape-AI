Destroyers

Destroyers are currently the most powerful ships available to players and are the backbone of any serious multiplayer battle. They have much superior defenses, damage output, and versatility due to their extra subsystem slot compared to their corvette and frigate counterparts, but they lose most of their speed.

Destroyers need docking tubes to disembark at a station and have very high manufacturing costs, most of which come from vexnium mining and the acquisition of blueprints that require having the second highest military rank or obtained via the player market (currently disabled).
