## CORESEC

Peacekeeping

### Capital

The Citadel

### Systems controlled

171

(7 - 113 - 51)

### Ideology

Diplomatic neutralism

### Color(s)

Grey, white, and black


> *"The Core Systems Security Cooperation, known to most as CoreSec, is a non-governmental entity that operates the Citadel and monitors secure systems of the galaxy to facilitate diplomatic relations and ensure some semblance of order and peace for galactic citizens. It maintains a monopoly of the galaxy's coloning network, using the profits from the service to fund its operation."*  
> - The CoreSec's career tab description.

CoreSec is a neutral entity that primarily maintains and patrols the Inner Core and is the first faction introduced to a new player, controlling the Citadel. They are the diplomatic body that restricts the expansion of the warring factions through the delimitation of permanent claims and colonization zones.

CoreSec is responsible for much of Starscape's infrastructure, funding their operation and security forces:

* The monopoly on cloning technology.
* Responsible for salvaging destroyed player ships.
* Responsible for neutral stations.
* Ownership of 30% of the relay network.
* Responsible for the bounty database.

CoreSec's peacekeeping fleet exclusively patrols stations in the 6 core systems surrounding the Citadel. Although not explicitly mentioned in the game, the neutral ship lines were designed by this faction.

## Politics

CoreSec emphasizes its neutrality and prefers diplomatic measures to secure peace rather than open warfare. It is presumed to maintain friendly relations with the three warring factions, as well as with the Trade Union and the Mining Guild, as evidenced by the embassies and offices in the Citadel. CoreSec likely harbors a certain hostility towards the Syndicate, as the Syndicate's representative in the Citadel is forced to hide behind a plant to supposedly avoid CoreSec authorities. The only entities to which CoreSec demonstrates active hostility are primarily drones and pirates.
