Statistics

Statistics are the attributes of ships that show how effective a ship is in its specific role, whether in combat, exploration, or hauling, and the attribute of turrets, weapons, and subsystems shows how they function. Some rigs can also modify stats in specific situations.

## Ship Statistics

| Statistic | Indicates | Measurement | Improved by: |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| DPS | The sum of the DPS stat of all turrets equipped on a ship | Damage/second | Turret loadout |
| Alpha damage | The sum of the damage per shot stat of all turrets equipped on a ship | Damage | Turret loadout |
| Mining rate | The sum of the ore per minute stat of all turrets equipped on a ship | Ore/minute | Turret loadout |
| Shield | How many shield points does a ship have in total | Shield points | Hardlight Shields |
| Shield regen | The rate at which a ship recovers shield points | Shield points/second | Shield Amplifier, Shield Repairer |
| Deflection power | A reduction of the damage per shot of enemy turrets by 1 for every deflection unit when the shield is damaged | Deflection units | Enhanced Deflectors |
| Hull | How many hull points does a ship have in total | Hull points | Reinforced Hull |
| Hull regen | The rate at which a ship recovers hull points | Hull points/second | Patcher Nanobots, Hull Repairer |
| Armor rating | A reduction of the damage per shot of enemy turrets by 1 for every armor unit when the hull is damaged | Armor units | Composite Armor, Armor Boosters |
| Speed | The maximum possible speed of a ship | Meters/second | Skeletonized Chassis, Lightweight Engines, Afterburners, Overdrives |
| Agility | A ship's turning speed in a maximum turn | Degrees/second | Engine Gimbals, Ultralight Chassis |
| Acceleration | How fast a ship can go from one speed to another | Meters/second2 | Fuel Injector |
| Warp speed | The maximum speed of the warp field generated by a ship during region jumps | Astronomical Units/second | Warp Field Amplifier |
| Warp charge time | How long does a ship take to charge for an interstellar warp jump. | Seconds | Warp Charger |
| Energy capacity | The maximum energy storage of a ship | Megajoules | Battery |
| Energy regen | The rate at which a ship recovers energy | Megajoules/second | Parallel Circuits |
| Sensor strength | ? | Sensor strength units | Sensor Booster |
| Signal range | The distance from which other players' radars can detect a ship | Meters | Reflective Panels, Reflective Plating, Exhaust Restrictor |
| Cargo capacity | How many cargo items in total can a ship haul | Cargo slots | - |
| Turret slots | Which and how many turrets a ship has, or can equip | Number of F, P, S, M, and L turret slots | - |
| Subsystems | How many subsystems a ship can equip | Number of subsystem slots | - |
| Rig slots | Which types and how many rigs a ship can equip | Number of WEP, DEF, ENG, and RCT rig slots | - |

## Trivia

* The base DPS combat statistic of a warship uses tier I blasters and repeaters for its sum. Each PDT hardpoint adds 12.5 DPS, each small turret slot adds 42 DPS, and each medium turret slot adds 96 DPS. Megalodon warships' turret damage increase doesn't affect the displayed base DPS.
  + Calculating the DPS of the Sunfish (912), the only ship with large turrets that we know the stats of (even if they are not final), reveals that each large turret slot also adds 96 DPS.
* All ships have 0 hull regen by default.
