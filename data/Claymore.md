Claymore

*"The Claymore, an advanced upgrade of the successful Sabre chassis, is a hull tanking machine with remarkably strong damage output."*

The Claymore is a fighter-class ship of the Stronghold ship line. It is best suited as a bulky, heavy-hitting fighter, and is the upgraded version of the Sabre.

## Advantages

The Claymore has the highest durability and damage of any non-exotic fighter, making it a devastating ship in its class if shots manage to land. This makes it extremely effective in swarms, with a few dealing damage rivaling that of a warship.

## Disadvantages

The Claymore's durability comes at the cost of its agility. When engaged in direct combat, it is one of the easiest fighters to shoot, being targetable by even main turrets on warships. This means that while it is heavy hitting, it can easily be outmaneuvered and gunned down.

## Trivia

- The current Claymore is modeled by Jeff\_D.
