Phalanx

*"The Phalanx frigate is a sturdy and stubborn warship. Though it doesn't handle movement particularly well, it is perfectly content sitting in place while devastating its enemies."*

The Phalanx is a frigate-class ship of the Legionis ship line. It is best suited as a tanky fleet ship.

## Advantages

The Phalanx stands out for having defenses superior to some destroyers, being the only frigate that can face any NPC structure or fleet head-on, reducing the grind needed to access high-tier loot, making it one of the best ships for game progression.

## Disadvantages

Despite its niche in PVE, the Phalanx does not excel in PVP. Like the Echelon, its turret placement is poor, and it will never be able to use full DPS, firing only 3-4 turrets most of the time against any skilled player.

The Phalanx has the standard Legionis warship weaknesses: kinetic weapons and kiting; it also relies too much on hull repairers due to having a single subsystem slot.
