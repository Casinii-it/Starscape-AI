Ancient Beacon

![](./File:AncientBeaconIcon.png)

Ancient Beacons are salvage items added in the *Echoes of the Past* update (6/3/21). They are acquired in satellite X-structures, found only in wild space, and function as a map for a "treasure hunt" across the galaxy.

## Obtainment and Inspection

To obtain a beacon, players must interact with the access panel and complete the hacking minigame. After collecting the beacon, the satellite will initiate a 10-second self-destruct sequence, which does no damage to the player.

Inspecting the item in your inventory will display a small image of colored, connected dots, with a single highlighted dot. This image corresponds to a random area on the galaxy map, with the colours of the dots aligned with their spectral classes. The next step is going to the indicated star system.

## In The System

Upon exiting warp in the indicated system, a notice is given to the player:

> **Relics of the Past**
>
> *"Your computer sounds an alarm tone to indicate that the fire suppression system has been activated in the cargo hold. Apparently, the ancient beacon you were carrying with you has been vaporized.*
>
> *Upon further inspection, the beacon seems to have emitted a directed signal pulse just before self-destructing. Scans in that direction of space indicate the presence of a radiation source which would have been undetectable if you hadn't known(sic) exactly where to look for it.*
>
> *The coordinates of the anomalous reading have been added to your navigation computer."*

Then it will be revealed the location of a deadspace X-structure that appears as an aberration or, uniquely, as an anomaly if there are no ringed planets. Warping to the location will reveal an intact, active version of the destroyed X-structures, guarded by a group of ancient drones. Containers found there often contain: ancient-tier modules and turrets, as well as sets of 1000 korrelite, reknite, gellium, or metal scraps.
