Spectral Classes

![](./File:SpectralMap.png)

Starscape Map, in Spectral Class

Spectral class is a filter on the galaxy map that makes all star systems take on the 'color' of their main star. Previously, it was a feature without game implications, but after the *Echoes of the Past* update, it serves as a way for players to locate systems highlighted by ancient beacons.

### Stars

Stars are mainly cosmetic objects in Starscape, although they are the center of each star system, with 1 to 3 found in each. The star details in the system map include their classification according to the Morgan-Keenan system, which determines their spectral class.
