Cruisers

![The legacy cruisers (front) and their current models (back).](./File:New_vs_Old_Cruisers.PNG)

The legacy cruisers (front) and their current models (back).

Cruisers are a class of sub-capital ships above destroyers, being larger and more powerful. Currently, they are not available to players and are only seen in use by NPCs of the Foralkan Empire, the Kavani Mandate, and the Lycentian Federation, by drones, or by developers (specifically the Sunfish).

| **Line** | **Cruiser** |
| --- | --- |
| Mandator | Subjugator |
| Megalodon | Sunfish |
| Righteous | Dignity |

Faction variants can be found guarding their core systems, where they have the 'Imperial', 'Sovereign', or 'Federal' tag, and contested system HQs, and in patrol fleets. Drone variants can be found rarely in DH anomalies.

Legacy cruiser wrecks of the three military factions can still be seen in battlefields.
