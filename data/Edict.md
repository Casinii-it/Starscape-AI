Edict

*"The Edict corvette is a tough and agile warship. It is commonly used by the Kavani in patrol and rapid response fleets."*

The Edict is a corvette-class ship of the Mandator ship line. It is best suited as a fast tech-based ship due to its high energy and subsystem slots.

## Advantages

The Edict has impressive energy for a corvette, and can make good use of its subsystem slots. While the base stats of the Edict are not impressive alone, augmented with subsystems and rigs makes it a deadly ship in the hand of an aware pilot.

## Disadvantages

Due to the Edict's reliance on modules, it can be very expensive to replace if modules are lost in combat. In addition, it is more fragile compared to other corvette-class ships.

Because Edict is seen to be very agile and dodging many turrets easily, if the Edict get into a Stasis Field, its speed and agility would degrade and can get hit by turrets easier, depending on what tier this subsystem is in.

Additionally, because of it's top-heavy turret placement, the Edict has limited options to defend itself from attacks from below, and should be wary of fighters sneaking under it to minimize the amount of damage they take.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
