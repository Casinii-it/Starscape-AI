> - The Foralkan Empire's career tab description.

The Foralkan Star Empire, located on the southeastern side of the Core, based in the Empyrean Highlands and maintaining full sovereignty over Harvest and half of Passage, is one of the three warring factions in Starscape. This nation possesses the smallest permanent territory among its rivals, but it is also the only one with more secure than unsecure systems, fighting with the Lycentian Federation for control over Schism, and the Kavani Mandate, over Valence.

The slang term for this nation is "Fork," an incorrect abbreviation of Foralkan, or "Fish," due to the names of the Megalodon ships.

## History

What is now Foralkus was once a vassal human colony on the fringes of the Federation. A rebellion led by Admiral, now Emperor, Hippaforalkus, aided by a sudden offensive from the Kavani, allowed both nations to grow beyond the shadow of Lycentia. The rapid expansion of the new Empire consolidated its place in galactic politics, and despite being now considered a threat by its former ally, Foralkus endures in the current war that takes place on Starscape.

The justification presented for the initial rebellion states that, during the golden age of the Lycentian Federation, the lack of opposition rendered it incapable of resisting future threats to humanity; thus, the Empire took this responsibility upon itself, be it from drones or its failed protectors. The ship designs of the Imperial Armada reflect this desire to eliminate the weaknesses they perceived in the Federation; Megalodon ships are designed for siege combat (i.e., structure destruction), built to destroy what came before and then rebuild based on the Empire's values of strength.
