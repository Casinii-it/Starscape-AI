## Overview

Stardust is a secondary currency added in the Next Gen update. It is only used to purchase ship cosmetics and an alternate form of posting ads. Stardust has several more planned uses, including cybermods, exclusive outfits, and star-liners, which are collector ships that have very little armament, but can carry other players.

## Ways of obtaining

Currently, there are 2 ways of obtaining stardust: either playing the game everyday to claim a daily reward of 5 stardust or purchasing some with robux. You will have to wait 23 hours and 30 minutes until the next daily reward.

Stardust Prices, in Robux:

* 100 RBX: 100 Stardust
* 475 RBX: 500 Stardust
* 900 RBX: 1000 Stardust
* 1700 RBX: 2000 Stardust
* 4000 RBX: 5000 Stardust

## The Stardust Shop

The Stardust Shop is where players spend their Stardust. It contains 5 tabs: Featured, Cosmetics, Starliners, Cybermods and Outfits. Only the Featured and Cosmetics tabs have content.

![](./File:Stardust_Purchase_Tab.png)

The Featured Tab

* Cosmetics

This tab has 3 sections:

* The Featured Bundle section where players can buy limited time bundles. As the Pirate Collection is currently the only bundle, it is permanently displayed here.
* The Weekly Deals where cosmetics can be bought at a greater discount than daily deals and are reset every week.
* The Daily Deals where cosmetics can be bought at a discount and are reset everyday.

On this tab, players can inspect all the purchasable ship cosmetics and purchase them at default price. Purchased cosmetics will be greyed out and covered with a green check mark.

## Trivia

* Weekly and Daily Deals reset at 12am UTC.
* The Pirate Bundle costs more than its individual components, making it a poor purchase, doubly so considering daily deals.
* Before the introduction of stardust, players would have to spend Robux directly to buy paid cosmetics.
* Paid cosmetics offer unique effects beyond what is possible with faction rewards. Skins have unique textures, and laser and trail colors are only obtainable through stardust.
* Buying stardust is the only way to spend Robux in Starscape.
