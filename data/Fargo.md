Fargo

*"The Fargo is an entry-level vendor, a ship with the ability to sell items directly to other pilots through its onboard store."*

The Fargo is a vendor-class ship of the Fargo line that can sell up to 4 items at a time in the same way that Trade Union and Syndicate ships sell items in space.

## Advantages

The Fargo provides a unique way to sell items to other players. Selling items from a Fargo offers higher visibility than placing an item on the market while also being less hassle than a direct trade. If the marketplace is taken offline, vendors become the best option to sell items.

## Disadvantages

The Fargo has no weaponry of any kind, leaving it completely unable to defend itself. It's also very slow, with poor acceleration, low top speed, and slow warp speed. It's a less capable vendor than the Yuma, with 2 display screens instead of 4 and less than half the item capacity.

## Trivia

* The crafting recipe was removed in the Next Gen Update, but was later added back Version 11.2.0, along with the Yuma.
* Because it has no weapons whatsoever, its weapon rig slot is completely useless.
* The current Fargo is modeled by Jeff\_D.
* Ever since all trading was disabled to counter a dupe glitch (Sep-Oct 2025), this ship (along with it's T2 brother) will not allow players to purchase items from it.
