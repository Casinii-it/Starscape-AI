Ship Lines

Ship lines are groups of ships that share a design philosophy, stat orientation, and obtainment methods. There are 2 main types of ship lines:

* Neutral ship lines: ships that are aways available at shipyard terminals for crafting.
  + Used by pirates and NPC combat ships from CoreSec, ITU, MG and The Syndicate.
* Faction ship lines: ships that require a blueprint purchased with loyalty points after reaching a certain level of reputation or military rank in some faction to be crafted.
  + Only used by NPC ships from their respective factions.

## Trivia

* Although dreadnoughts do not exist in the game, combat ships lines are named after their dreadnought. For example, the Constellation line's dreadnought is called Constellation.
* Industrial ship lines are usually refered to by the name of their lightest ship, with the exception of the Everest and Mastodon lines.
  + The Nebula, Cloak, and Mirage lines are named after ships that never made it into the game, as shown in this concept video by swindle150.
    - The Koronis is the only starship in the concept video that was added to Starscape.
