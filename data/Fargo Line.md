Fargo Line

The Fargo Line features currently all the ships of the vendor class, which are used to sell items to other players, similar to freighters. However, these ships don't have any way of defending themselves, having zero turrets despite having a weapon rig slot.

To list an item, access your inventory menu. On the right side of the menu, there will be a listing menu where you will be able to list any item in your inventory.

|  | Vendor |
| --- | --- |
| T1 | Fargo |
| T2 | Yuma |

## Trivia

* This line was 'removed' in the Next Gen update. Players who already had them, kept them, but they were uncraftable and therefore only obtainable from other players.
  + This line was re-added on December 16, 2024 along with updated models.
