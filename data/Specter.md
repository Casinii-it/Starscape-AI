Specter

*"The Specter is a nimble, stealthy mining corvette developed by the Syndicate for mining smaller quantities of ore quickly before fleeing from the scene."*

The Specter is a stealth-miner of the Mirage ship line, manufactured by the Syndicate faction.

## Advantages

- The Specter moves significantly faster than other mining corvettes.

- It also has a significantly lower signal range than other miners.

## Disadvantages

- The specter has a kneecapped mining rate compared to the ozark, making it generally worse than the ozark as a viable mining ship.

## Trivia

* The current Specter is modeled by swindle150.
