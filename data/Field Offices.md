Field Offices

*Notice: using the Agent Finder tool on the sidebar of the galaxy map, it is possible to check for the location of all field offices.*

Field office station modules, usually found in non-neutral stations in systems in the faction's permanent territory, are rooms with several NPCs wearing their respective uniforms and a circular table in the middle of the field office, glowing with the primary color of their faction. Field offices have the following functions:

* 1-2 field agents that can assign missions to players.
* A faction terminal that sells standing tier and military rank rewards.
* Kavani, Foralkan, and Lycentian field offices each have one recruiter NPC.

## Trivia

* Tier 0 to 3 field agents of the warring factions use tier 2 uniforms (Commander/Preafect), and tier 4 to 5 field agents use tier 3 uniforms (Admiral/Marshal).
  + Tier 0 to 3 CoreSec field agents use tier 1 uniforms, but tier 4 to 5 field agents use different uniforms unavailable to players, with their shades of grey inverted.
* Field agents of all tiers from the economic factions use the same uniform.
