X-Structures

![](./File:Bgxs.png)

One of the few X-structures in-game from a far view, this one spawned outside a ring, but due to in-game bugs, another ring and asteroid field are generated.

X-Structure is one category of aberration that consists of derelict space structures with ancient drone wreckages and secure containers placed around them. They can be found on any planet of a PvP-enabled system, but they are much more common on ringed planets and in wild systems.

If you are looking for the intact X-structures, check the page for ancient beacons.

### Loot

The only source of loot in X-structures is the secure containers, which can store metal scraps, ores, minerals, and, very rarely, ancient-tier turrets and modules. X-structures are converted into deadspace by fully looting all of their containers.

## Known Variants

### All Planets

* **Hub/Storage Bay -** These are the most common structures, always with 3 containers. They are ring-shaped structures, with 4 “arms” extending upwards.

* **Shipyard -** These are common structures, always with 4 containers. They are made up of the wreckage of an unusual ship, not found anywhere else in-game, which crashed in a shipyard formed by a service structure and a tower.

* **Ancient Relay Satellite -** These are the rarest structures, featuring a single container with an ancient beacon and self-destructing after being looted (approximately 0.25% to 0.2% chance of finding). They resemble man-made satellites, with 16 solar panels and a parabolic antenna.

### Only Ringed Planets

* **Single-Ringed Station (Colossus) -** These are rare structures, always with 4 containers. They have a central tower with a single ring around it, supported by 4 glass bridges.

* **Double-Ringed Station (DualColossus) -** These are taller variants of the Colossus, with an additional ring and a lengthier tower. They always have 5 containers.

* **Large Single-Ringed Station (Behemoth) -** These are very rare structures, always with 6 containers. Compared to the Colossus, the tower and ring are much wider and elongated, with much more detail.

## Trivia

* There is no information about the origin of these structures.
* Only "ringed stations" and satellites have an intact variant in-game.
  + Ancient relay satellites don't have an already destroyed variant in-game at all.
* Satellites are the only X-structures without ancient drone wrecks around them.
* The wrecked ship in the shipyard structure appears to use docking ports, similar to destroyers, and also circular engines that current in-game ships use.
