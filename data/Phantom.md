Phantom

*"The Phantom is a more capable and larger stealth mining vessel than the smaller Specter, opting for more defense and yield while trying to maintain discreetness and agility."*

The Phantom is a stealth-miner of the Mirage ship line, manufactured by the Syndicate faction.

## Advantages

* Very low sensor range: just half of the Kodiak's. Combined with its high agility across the board, as well as having two slots for engine rigs, it is trivial for a Phantom to slip out of the fingers of any threat.
* Unlike other miners, the Phantom does not have a debuff to turret damage, allowing it to pack a decent punch for its class when considering its advantage of two turret slots over the Kodiak.

## Disadvantages

* Mediocre turret placement makes any combat use very questionable, and also makes mining at certain angles particularly difficult.
* Unlike the Kodiak, the Phantom requires the addition of a blueprint in its recipe: something that can only be obtained by either obtaining sufficient Syndicate reputation, or buying a blueprint off of the marketplace.
* Only has a 75% bonus to mining rate as opposed to the Kodiak's 100%, making the Phantom the less efficient miner of the two.
