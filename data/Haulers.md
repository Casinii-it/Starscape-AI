Haulers

Haulers are industrial ships that come in a variety of sizes, sharing the same focus on carrying cargo. They are used for spice trading, supplying both contested systems and player structures, and missions that reward commerce skill points. However, they are far worse at defending themselves from players and NPCs for their low amount of turrets and turret damage debuff in 2 of the 3 hauler lines.
