Freighters are NPC ships associated with either the Trade Union or the Syndicate. By pressing F at a minimum distance of 1 km from the freighter, the trader window opens and allows players to purchase from a collection of 4 to 5 different items that the freighters are hauling.

### Spawn Locations

* Trade Union freighters: found at warp hubs and rings of any secure, contested, and unsecure system.
* Syndicate freighters: found at warp hubs and rings of any PvP-enabled system.
* *Defend the Transport* and *Rescue Operation* mission sites, replacing faction transport.

## Behavior

Freighters can be seen warping in if a player is already observing the spot where the freighter spawned. Otherwise, a freighter will be visible to the player upon exiting warp.

These ships function mostly like faction fleet ships: freighters will shoot back in self-defense, and players lose 10 standing with the faction the freighter belonged to if they destroy one. However, freighters will attempt to warp out if they begin taking hull damage and have a very small chance of dropping some of the items they were selling.

Reaching Tier II and Tier IV standing with the Trade Union and the Syndicate grants a discount for items sold by their freighters of 10% and 16.6%, respectively.
