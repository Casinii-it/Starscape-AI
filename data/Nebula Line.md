Nebula Line

The Nebula Line, utilized by the Trade Union, features the haulers with the largest cargo capacity in the game. However, all of its field stats are severely reduced and the rig's focus on defense and attack don't help Nebula ships fulfill their role, in addition to the line's passive reduction in turret damage.

| T1 | Astero | Galaxy | Andromeda |

## Trivia

* The Nebula line ships are named after stars or groups of stars.
  + Nebulae are clusters of gas that serve as the birthplace of stars.
