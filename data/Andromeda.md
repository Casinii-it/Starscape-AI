Andromeda

*"The Andromeda is a massive freighter, utilized by the Trade Union for transporting bulk cargo when danger is not of large concern or it has sufficient escort. Although it is practically stationary in terms of speed and agility, its cargo capacity is in a league of its own."*

The Andromeda is a massive hauler of the Nebula line. It was added as a player ship in the Conquered Frontier update, but was used as the Trade Union freighter for some time before that.

## Advantages

* The Andromeda is the ship with the largest cargo capacity in the game, greatly reducing the time invested in supplying structures in contested systems.
* This hauler is capable of defending itself from NPC attackers and possibly from player fightcraft with its improved firepower.

## Disadvantages

* The Andromeda is extremely slow, making it an easy target for player pirates.
* The Andromeda features a -20% damage penalty, making even self-defense victory difficult and unlikely.
* As a Destroyer, it is extremely expensive to make and to lose (specially in wild space with WH-FO spice trading).
* The Andromeda costs more than other destroyers to build, being the same amount as the Yukon.
* As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
