NPC Marketplace

![](./File:Marketplace.png)

Legacy Citadel marketplace.

Marketplaces are station modules that allow players to buy from a selection of items, sell items, and list items in the local player marketplace by accessing the market terminal.

There are 5 types of marketplace modules: General Market, Weapon Dealer, Ship Vendor, Marketplace (exclusive to the Citadel), and Furniture Emporium (only found in residential stations). Their design is the same except for the color of the lights, the terminal, and the items displayed in the banners; Furniture Emporiums are unique because they display the items they sell in the room.

If more than one marketplace module is in a station, their item selections will be combined, and this same selection will be accessible from all market terminals in that station. This doesn't work with Furniture Emporiums, but the General Market, which is also always present in residential stations, does sell furniture.

## NPC Market

NPC market refers to their permanent item selections, which depend on the types of marketplace modules in the station and the fixed purchasing value from which they buy items from players.

They are very useful for newer players, offering entry-level ships and low-tier weapons, armor, and turrets, and serve as a source of starting credits.
