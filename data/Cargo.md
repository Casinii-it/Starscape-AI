Cargo

Cargo are items that can only be hauled by ships with available cargo slots and stored in cargo docks of NPC stations (500 cargo maximum per station). Cargo occupies 500m3 by default. When ejecting or dropping cargo (not including 'inventory items'), each cargo item is dropped in individual containers.
