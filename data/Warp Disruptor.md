Warp Disruptor

*This is the deployable warp disruptor. For the subsystem, see Subsystems.*

## Warp Disruptor

## Basic Info

### Effects

Prevents ships within its radius from warping until it's either destroyed or its duration runs out.

### Source

Tier I: Marketplace, Pirate Dens, Crafting  
Tier II and III: Pirate Dens, Crafting

### Cost to buy

Tier I: 500  
Tier II and III: unbuyable

### Sell value

Tier I: 250  
Tier II: 375  
Tier III: 500

## Statistics

### Disruption range

Tier I: 3000m  
Tier II: 3500m  
Tier III: 4000m

### Duration

Tier I, II and III: 3600 seconds (1 hour)

### Activation delay

Tier I: 5 seconds  
Tier II: 3 seconds  
Tier III: 2 seconds

### Hull points

Tier I: 1500  
Tier II: 2000  
Tier III: 2500

### Deploy cooldown

Tier I, II and III: 30 seconds



A **warp disruptor** is an item that can be bought at the marketplace and can be salvaged from pirate dens. When deployed, this item will create a large sphere that will prevent any ship from warping while it's in the sphere. This item is useful for preventing players or AI-controlled ships from warping away during combat.

There are currently 3 Tiers of Warp disruptors in the game, each tier having higher stats than the previous.

Warp Disruptor deployables only work in Wild Systems.
