Luster

*"The Luster is a Lycentian fighter. This all-around ship is a step ahead of the Aurora which it is derived from."*

The Luster is a T1 fighter-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a tanky fighter, similar in stats to the Aurora but with improved agility.

## Advantages

The Luster's high shield pool and agility puts it on par with the Aurora despite being a cheap faction fighter, and allows it to do high-level maneuvers that other heavy fighters would not be able to do, while retaining a respectable damage rate. In addition, it's defense rigs lets players further enhance their shield pool.

## Disadvantages

The Luster is an T1 faction fighter, and thus will perform poorly against its upgraded counterparts. In addition, a low hull pool causes it to be vulnerable to ion-class weapons due to its shield focus. Despite being more agile than the Aurora, it is still easily outmaneuvered by interceptors or ships with an engine focus.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
