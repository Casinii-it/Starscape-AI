Polaris

*"The Polaris is an advanced upgrade of the Zenith design that generally improves upon its speed, offense, and defense."*

The Polaris is an interceptor-class ship of the Valiance ship line. It is best suited as an agile but deadly fighter-interceptor combination, and is the upgraded version of the Zenith.

## Advantages

The Polaris offers the absurd agility and speed of an interceptor compared to other starships, and packs a punch due to its combat-focused rig layout, creating a deadly fighter-interceptor combo.

## Disadvantages

The Polaris is more capable at dealing damage, but its reduced agility puts this fighter at a disadvantage when trying to disengage compared to the Paragon. Its combat-focused rig layout sacrifices the tackle and scout abilities of its counterpart.

## Trivia

- The current Polaris is modeled by Jeff\_D.

- Before the Next Gen update, the Polaris was one of the only ships with more shield then hull.
