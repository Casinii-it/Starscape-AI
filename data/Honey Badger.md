Honey Badger

*"The Honey Badger takes the ore extraction rate of the Badger and dials it up even further, creating a sturdy and extremely efficient mining ship."*

The Honey Badger is a miner-class ship of the Badger ship line. It is best suited as a bulk mining ship.

## Advantages

The Honey Badger is still reasonably cheap compared to warship miners, allowing it to get in spaces larger ships could not. It is the most powerful small miner in terms of mining efficiency.

The Honey Badger also excels at 'cherry picking' - a form of mining which involves only mining rare ores such as Red Narcor, Narcor, or Axnit.

## Disadvantages

The Honey Badger is tanky but slow, and will die to pirates if it is caught- like most ships. Its PDTs could lend it some self-defense, but they are usually used for more convenient tractor turrets.
