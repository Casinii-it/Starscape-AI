Legionis Line

The Legionis line focuses on defensive capabilities and playstyle is generally geared towards PvE. Ships are very accessible to players, as they do not require blueprints, despite the higher material cost compared to other warships. Warships have lower mobility to compensate for having defenses that surpass those of superior classes; however, fighters don't have any particularly weak stat, being average to above average across the board while still maintaining high defenses.

Some of the common traits of the ships are:

- High hull and armor for warships, and shield and deflection for fighters, which significantly reduces the threat of NPCs;

- High energy capacity allows for greater use of subsystems, but mainly repairers and boosters;

- Low mobility in warships decreases PVP viability by making them extremely vulnerable to kiting, as well as unable to disengage from fights;

- Low shielding in warships creates a great weakness to kinetic weapons, particularly to railguns, which mostly ignore their high armor rating.
