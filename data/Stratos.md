Stratos

*"The Stratos is an old but reliable scout ship that was produced in massive quantities in the later years of human galactic colonization."*

The Stratos is an entry-level starter-class ship of the Constellation ship line. All players begin with this ship, and a new one is given to the player for free whenever it is destroyed.

## Advantages

The Stratos is unable to be permanently lost, and will instantly be refunded to players upon death.

This makes it a great scout or travel ship if you are concerned about ambushes or death.

## Disadvantages

The Stratos is pitifully weak, which makes sense due to it being the starter ship. However, it still has the necessary firepower to do low-level missions.

## Trivia

* The current Stratos is modeled by swindle150.
* Starter ships other than the Stratos were planned to be released in the Next Generation update, but were scrapped due to time limitations. As such, the Stratos remains the only Scout-class ship.
* The Stratos cannot be crafted or repackaged.
* The Stratos is the only ship in Starscape where kills against it are recorded in every player's career.
* For a 2021 April Fools event, the Stratos' size was drastically increased to frigate proportions, and many were utilized against a few of the developers (who were in *Sunfish* class Foralkan cruisers, equipped with full Flak Cannon V) in an epic battle, resulting in the triumph of the Stratos. <https://m.youtube.com/watch?v=9WWdPZzMck4>
* After the Next Gen update, various players were able to destroy the Drone Battleship with the Stratos, utilizing long-range pulse hardpoints and the battleship's bugged aggro range to snipe it without retaliation.
