Miner Turret

*This article is about warship miner turrets. Check the Fighter Hardpoints article for information on the mining turrets in small ships.*

![](./File:Ozark_Mining_Lasers.png)

An Ozark mining with two Mining Turrets.

Miner turrets are item turrets that can be equipped on warships to acquire ores from asteroids more efficiently. These turrets deal no damage, neither when fired at nor flown through by other ships. The only weapon rig that affects miner turrets is enhanced servos.

## Usage

When fired, they project a small laser that turns green or red depending on whether an asteroid is detected or not. If an asteroid is detected, then they will fire a mining beam, which varies in color depending on the turret type, and will begin to mine the asteroid until the asteroid is depleted or until the player makes them stop.

As it mines, a portion of the ore will be automatically retrieved through the beam to your inventory based on the capture ratio stat, and a portion of it will break off of the asteroid and need to be tractored in manually through the use of PDTs.

## Types of Turrets

4 types of mining turrets that can be manufactured, with varying levels of performance and speed. The regular miner turret branches into 3 separate lines with different focuses, and their final tier requires a blueprint obtained with faction standing from the Mining Guild.

|  |  |  |
| --- | --- | --- |
|  | Miner Turret | Regular miner turrets are the first ones players have access to and are more convenient than the mining hardpoints of capsule miners. However, they have the lowest efficiency of all and should be replaced as soon as players have access to water to craft heat sinks. |
|  | Precision Miner | These miners' high capture ratio makes them ideal for afk mining in deposit aberrations, where asteroids have large ore reserves. By dividing your beams between asteroids, it is possible to be away for several minutes before they are depleted, no tractors required. It is not recommended for exploration and cherry-picking, as it's **extremely** time-inefficient. |
|  | Ice Miner | Although most useful when just mining water ice in already explored systems, its relatively high release rate when fully upgraded also allows for general use while grinding for large amounts of water and heavy water. |
|  | Strip Miner | For the most part, the most effective mining turret. Players will be able to quickly clear systems of their desired resources or turn any asteroid field anomaly into deadspace. |
