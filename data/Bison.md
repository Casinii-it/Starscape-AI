Bison

*"The Bison is a hauler designed to have a large cargo capacity at the expense of speed and agility. It won't outrun anything, but it can make you a fortune if you manage to make it to your destination."*

The Bison is a hauler-class ship of the Mastodon line. It is best suited as a fast and cheap hauler ship.

## Advantages

The Bison is a cheap upgrade from the Hyrax, and has a respectable cargo capacity, making it ideal for early-game missions where you have to supply many items or faction supply hauling with a disposable ship. Despite being slower than other fighter-style haulers, it still outpaces ships like the Zubron or the Galaxy.

## Disadvantages

The Bison has a large cargo capacity for its class, but still has less than half the cargo capacity of the Zubron. For more advanced pilots, warship haulers hit the Bison out of the park, with sometimes triple the cargo capacity and yet more speed and durability.
