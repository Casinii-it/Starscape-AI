Enemy Presence

![](./File:RobloxScreenShot20201005_172319477.png)

A Radix fighting the Drone Commander of a Drone Fleet.

Enemy presence, commonly referred to as fleets, is a large group of hostile NPCs that patrol a warp hub or ring. They have 2 to 5 waves, with more waves meaning that more powerful ships will appear. Players may encounter places with the presence of drones or pirates.

## Mechanics

### Waves

When warping into a planet with the presence of drones or pirates, ship icons will appear at the top of the player's screen, one for each wave. The final wave, indicated by a star icon, will spawn with a command ship, a heavily buffed ship. To complete a wave, the player must destroy all ships associated with the event, or, in the case of the final wave, destroy the command ship, completing the event.

Once a wave has been completed, a new wave of enemy ships will warp in. Waves that have been successfully cleared will appear as a checkmark icon in the top GUI. The current wave will have a green progress bar that rises to the top as the remaining ships are destroyed. The number of remaining ships in the current wave is also listed at the top in text form.

### Enemy Presence Levels

* 2-wave **Fireteams** will spawn a squad leader on the final wave. Only found in secure systems.
* 3-wave **Squadrons** will spawn a squad leader or a lieutenant on the final wave. Found in secure, contested, and unsecure systems.
* 4-wave **Fleets** will spawn a lieutenant or a commander on the final wave. Found in PVP-enabled systems.
* 5-wave **Armadas** will spawn a commander or a captain on the final wave. Only found in wild systems.

#### *SCANNER WARNING - A high-energy signature is closing in on your position.*

* 10% of the time, after defeating the command ship of a **drone armada**, all remaining drones will warp out, and a sixth wave will begin, where a drone battleship will warp in, escorted by elite drone destroyers and frigates. For more information, see Drone Battleship.

## Tips

1. Remember that more enemy ships only spawn at the start of a new wave. You can take your time kiting and healing without losing your progress.

2. If the warp hub you are fighting in has an asteroid field or wreckage field, avoid going through them, as there are usually loose drones or pirates roaming around, which not only means more enemies firing at you, but also that you will waste time destroying them.

3. Before the final wave arrives, make sure to stay away from the warp-in area, as substantially stronger ships will spawn escorting the command ship. After that, you can proceed in two ways: shoot only the command ship and try to lure it to leave the fleet and come after you alone, or try to take out the escorts first, also luring them one by one.

## Trivia

* Squad leaders use either fighters or interceptors, lieutenants use corvettes, commanders use frigates, and captains use destroyers.
  + Drone admirals (command drone cruisers) exist, but can currently only be spawned by a developer.
