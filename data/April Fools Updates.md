April Fools Updates

## 2021

![](./File:April_fools_update_.png)

Game icon on April 1st, 2021

For April Fools, the size of the Stratos was significantly increased. The official discord server also changed its banner to a Stratos. On the day, people went to Gatinsir to find Keth and other moderators and admins flying cruisers, which were being teased.

## 2022

![](./File:AprilF2022_2.png)

The update included an updated Roblox game icon. The Citadel's center now has four buttons. The buttons' tooltip says "Suspicious Button". Pressing it will enter the player into the queue of a mock Among Us game, taking place on the Citadel. Once in the game, players are assigned as crew or saboteurs. Saboteurs must kill all the crew, and the crew must complete the tasks within the time limit. There is no reward for winning.
