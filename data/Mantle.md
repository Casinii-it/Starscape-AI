Mantle

*"The Mantle is a massive freighter that sacrifices some cargo capacity for improved defenses and agility compared to other freighters. It is the Syndicate's ship of choice for running bulk cargo through areas of space."*

The Mantle is the biggest ship and Hauler used by the Syndicate. It was added as a player ship in the Conquered Frontier update, although it had existed ingame as the Syndicate Freighter for some time beforehand. Like the previous Haulers of the Syndicate the Mantle too is of the Cloak Line of ships, making it best suited as a fast and stealthy Hauler.

## Advantages

* The Mantle is bigger than the Shroud and Galaxy, allowing for more profit.
* Compared to the Andromeda, the Mantle has a significantly lower Signal Range and faster Warp speed allowing it to more-likely outrun Player Pirates.
* With a 36 cargo capacity, the Mantle is capable of supplying most build-able structures by itself with three or four runs.
* The Mantle is capable of defending itself from potential pirates thanks to the lack of damage debuffs, along with its excellent turret placement. However, combat in the Mantle is still recommended to be limited to self-defense.
* 6 small turret slots allows for respectable damage against fighters and corvettes.
* Decent health pool, especially for a Hauler.
* Excellent choice of rigs — escape is a trivial task for a competent Mantle pilot.

## Disadvantages

* Compared to the Andromeda, the Mantle, loses a significant amount of cargo space resulting in overall less profit.
* It is unsuited for combat and is generally useless against ships in its class.
* Thanks to its large cargo capacity, it is a juicy target for pirates.
* As a Destroyer, it is extremely expensive to make.
* As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

## Trivia

* The current Mantle is modeled by swindle150.
* First teased/shown on the 14th of April 2021 on Discord by swindle150 as a concept.
