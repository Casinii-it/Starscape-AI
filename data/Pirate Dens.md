Pirate Dens

![](./File:MediumPirateDen.png)

*Medium Pirate Den.*

Pirate dens are structures guarded by numerous pirates. They can spawn in all non-core systems, in warp hubs or rings. They resemble a group of round gray asteroids connected by metal trusses, and with various objects sticking out.

### Mechanics

Similar to drone factories, dens are made up of three different types of structures: shield generators, power regulators, and the main pirate den. The shield generators must be destroyed in order to damage the power regulators, and the power regulators must be destroyed in order to damage the main pirate den.

A wave of pirates will warp in upon the destruction of each shield generator and power regulator, and additional waves will warp in over time.

Pirate den wrecks may contain: credits, materials, deployables, salvage items, ship blueprints, and dread modules and turret blueprints.

## Den Sizes

There are three sizes of pirate dens: small, medium, and large. The bigger the den, the more hitpoints its parts have, the stronger the reinforcements become, and the higher the chance of obtaining dread loot.

## Combat Strategies

* While any warship and some fighters are capable of destroying small and medium dens quite easily, defensive frigates and destroyers are best for taking on large dens, specially those from the Righteous line, since most pirates use kinetic weaponry.
* It is possible to prevent the arrival of reinforcement fleets by logging (either through safelog or leaving the system and re-entering), as the game saves the status of the structure (e.g., destroyed parts) but not the reinforcement fleets spawned from their destruction.
* If you have long-range turrets and are willing to wait, it’s possible to use their extended range to cheese the den by evading detection by the pirates.
* it's often better to focus the frigates and corvettes first over any dread destroyers by agrooing them away from the den. The destroyers are slow and typically hang close to the nest itself - frigates are much more aggressive however, and can deal a lot of damage if you're not prepared.
