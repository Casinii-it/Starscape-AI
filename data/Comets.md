Anomaly:CM

![](./File:Comet_field.png)

A comet with its trail of ice cores and barren fragments.

The **CM Anomaly**, also known as **Comet** or **Comet Field**, is an Anomaly that contains a large comet with a trail of smaller asteroids. It is one of the known source of obtaining Water and Heavy Water, the other being in I class deposits.

## Topography and Usage

![](./File:Ice_core.png)

An ice core, with a barren fragment behind it.

CM Anomalies can be found in all systems except Core, being indicated as "CM" on the map. As with all other Anomalies, it must first be tracked by a Scanning Probe (though Squad warp-in feature can bypass this for fellow squad members, as with all other Anomalies & Aberrations). The anomaly contains a large comet, with a blueish white trail containing many smaller rocks. Some contain Ice, and can be recognized from a distance by light-blue patches of ice on their surface. Other rocks are barren fragments that contain no useful material and cannot be mined.

Ice Cores and Heavy Ice Cores can be mined using any mining method, but normal mining lasers and turrets will burn a significant portion of the ice. Efficient extraction requires Ice Miner Turrets, though these require water to craft. Water is required for crafting Fuels as well as Heat Sinks, whilst Heavy Water is required for crafting High-grade & Siege Fuels.

Like all asteroid anomalies, the anomaly will disappear once all the ice has been mined. If an anomaly seems devoid of ice cores, another player has already mined out the comet but missed an ice core.

As with the I-Aberration, a CM anomaly may contain Heavy Water cores in Wild systems. CM is typically rare, however it is near-guaranteed in the Alteran Reach region due to its Abundant Water buff. This buff also affects the spawn rate of I-Aberrations.

No Drones or other hostile ships spawn in a CM anomaly, and thus they can be used as a safe haven from hostile NPCs.

## Trivia

* This anomaly type was introduced in version 1.6.0 as part of the Faction Warfare update.
* Since the Conquered Frontiers update, CM received a buff that can yield over 1k Water Ice, without combining them with Heavy Water Ice yields. This buff is particularly noticeable in Alteran Reach due to abundant water in the region.
* The rare and unusable turret; Prospector was initially designed to scan all comet rocks for ice and barren fragments, however that feature was later scraped with the turrets being left in game.
