Megalodon Line

The Megalodon line, engineered by the Foralkan Empire, has a clear design choice: maximizing DPS while usually leaving gaping holes in other stats.

### Starships

Starships in this line have the most extreme stat distributions in the game, sacrificing any medium- or long-term survival with the sole purpose of destroying their target as quickly as possible, creating a high skill floor for these ships.

* Highest DPS and second highest mobility among interceptors.
* Highest agility and tied for highest DPS among fighters.
* Most fragile ships in the game, with the lowest amount of hitpoints in Starscape going to the Mackerel and Candiru, with 300 total (100 less than the Stratos).

### Warships

Warships in this line are slower, but have the highest damage output thanks to their innate turret damage boost, being the best option for sieging contested systems and player structures.

* The Knifejaw and the Barracuda have striking similarities with the Legionis warships: the same turret placement, but rotated 90° longitudinally, a high hull pool, and armor rating; they are also the only corvette and frigate with a fifth rig slot.
* The Loxodon has an even greater focus on DPS in detriment of most other stats, becoming a glass cannon.

### Turret Placement

Warships have a unique design choice: their main turrets are on the sides, so the focal points of the turrets are concentrated vertically, which creates its share of problems.

* Lower agility makes being flanked very dangerous for Megalodon warships, because failing to fully use their main asset (damage) is fatal.
* Monitors are usually wider than they are tall, creating a disadvantage compared to having the main turrets on the top and bottom of the ship (such as the Chevron and Justice) due to their larger field of view.
* It's very difficult to aim directly up or down due to camera movement limitations in Roblox.

## Trivia

* This is the ship line used by the Foralkan Imperial Armada.
* The Megalodon line ships are named after various fish species that live (or lived) on Earth.
  + The design of the warships also are made to resemble fish, having a proeminent front that looks like a fin.
