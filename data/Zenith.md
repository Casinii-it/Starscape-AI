Zenith

*"The Zenith is an agile starship developed to be able to hold its own in combat with normal fighters."*

The Zenith is an entry-level interceptor-class ship of the Valiance ship line. It is best suited as an agile but deadly fighter-interceptor combination.

## Advantages

The Zenith has much more speed compared to fighters such as the Sabre or Aurora, and can fly circles around them with the proper loadout. In addition, it can take and give out a respectable amount of damage despite being an entry-level interceptor.

## Disadvantages

The Zenith is an entry-level ship, and thus will perform poorly against upgraded fightercraft. As an interceptor itself, warships with turret hardpoints or point defense will be able to gun it down quite easily as well.
