Mako

*"An advanced upgrade of the Marlin, the Mako has an improved speed and mining rate over its predecessor."*

The Mako is a miner-class ship of the Marlin ship line. It is best suited as a fast mining ship.

## Advantages

The Mako is still reasonably cheap compared to warship miners, allowing it to get in spaces larger ships could not. With two engine slots, it can greatly reduce its signal range or warp charge time, making it good at mining in more dangerous zones such as near NPC structures. The Mako also boasts the fastest warp speed in the game.

## Disadvantages

The Mako is fragile due to its durability being traded for its speed, and a stray volley can easily destroy it. Its PDTs lend it some self-defense, but they are usually used for more convenient tractor turrets.
