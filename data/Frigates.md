Frigates

Frigates are the middle ground between the lighter and heavier classes, being at least viable against most other ships. The slower, defensive frigates can engage in late-game PvE, while the faster frigates are much more dangerous as kiters due to their higher damage output and can still give chase if the situation calls for it, and since most have 4 PDTs, starships are not a big problem outside of fleet battles.
