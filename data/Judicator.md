Judicator

*"The Judicator frigate is a formidable warship of the Kavani Navy. Its top-heavy turret placement allows it to go head-to-head with other ships in its class, despite its relative structural weakness."*

The Judicator is a frigate-class ship of the Mandator ship line. It is best suited as a high-tech frigate that makes use of its subsystem abilities.

## Advantages

The Judicator's subsystem count makes it good for PvE, allowing it to warp out, back off, and heal in case it is overwhelmed. It is also acceptable in PvP, but to excel, the pilot must have good energy management. The Judicator is great at kiting either with railguns or beams.

## Disadvantages

The Judicator's top-heavy turret placement is a double-edged sword, creating a blind spot under the ship. It also has poor armour and deflection, and high-DPS destroyers built for the role can gun it down quickly. There are no weapon slots, so the only choice of getting more damage is upgrading turret tiers or switching to turrets with better dps.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

Due to its reliance on modules, the Judicator is expensive to replace if lost during combat.
