Koronis

*"The Koronis combines modest mining ability with the ability to haul cargo, allowing it to support itself on long-duration mining missions or take on multiple roles."*

The Koronis is a miner-class ship of the Koronis Line. It is best suited as a fast mining ship for long expeditions.

## Advantages

The Koronis is cheaper than warship miners, which allows it to try for more dangerous expeditions. With 2 cargo crates, and a good mix of weapon and engine rigs, it is a cheap but effective generalist miner.

## Disadvantages

Despite being a fighter-style miner, it requires a blueprint to build, so most experienced players opt for a warship miner such as the Eos or Ozark instead. Much like other fighter-style miners, it is fragile.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

## Trivia

* The current Koronis is modeled by Ocean\_tsunamii.
* The Koronis is the only ship whose blueprint can be acquired from more than one faction.
