Halo

*"The Halo frigate, named for the iconic ring of its midsection, utilizes a lightweight construction that makes it significantly faster than other frigates."*

The Halo is a frigate-class ship of the Constellation ship line. It is best suited as a fast exploration ship due to its rigs and speed.

## Advantages

The Halo is good at exploration due to its engine rigs, allowing it to warp around quickly and deal with threats from afar. As a frigate with several PDTs, it is reasonably efficient at NPC hunting as well, with main turrets dealing with large threats while its PDTs deal with small fightercraft.

## Disadvantages

The Halo, much like the rest of the Constellation line, is fragile, and if caught, will be killed quickly. While it can be used as a long-range kiting ship, it is outclassed by the Adamant. Fighters can chase this ship down despite its engine rigs if a pilot is not careful. Its healthpool is closer to the health of a corvette.
