*Drone Factory*.

Drone Factories are structures guarded by numerous drones. They can spawn in all non-core systems, in warp hubs or rings. Factories resemble a combination of the structures found in hideouts.

### Mechanics

Similar to pirate dens, factories are made up of three different types of structures: shield generators, power regulators, and the main reactor. The shield generators must be destroyed in order to damage the power regulators, and the power regulators must be destroyed in order to damage the main reactor.

A wave of drones will warp in upon the destruction of each shield generator and power regulator, and additional waves will warp in over time.

Drone factories wrecks may contain: high tier materials, salvage items, and hybrid modules and blueprints.

## Drone Factory Levels

There are four levels of drone factory: depot, foundry, factory and megacomplex. The higher the level, the more hitpoints its parts have, the stronger the reinforcements become, and the higher the chance of obtaining hybrid loot.

## Combat Strategies

* While most combat ships capable of destroying depots and foundries quite easily, highly defensive frigates and destroyers are best for taking on factories and megacomplexes, such as those of the Righteous and Legionis lines.

## Trivia

* Prior to the 1.7.0 update, factories were not destructible and would constantly spawn drones around it whose numbers were based on the factory tier. In addition, their primary color was white instead of black.
