Veil

*"The Veil is a stealth-focused hauling corvette. Although it has lower capacity than the Astero or Zubron, its high agility and low signal range make it ideal for hauling missions requiring speed and stealth."*

The Veil is a hauler-class ship of the Cloak line. It is best suited as a cheap but stealthy hauler.

## Advantages

The Veil is around the cost of a corvette similar to a Zubron, but is much faster and has a low signal range, causing it to be hard to find using sensors. The Veil excels as a solo hauling ships on dangerous routes, such as the frontlines of contested space or systems that have possible hostile threats.

## Disadvantages

The Veil is fragile if caught, and pilots should take care not to be hit by stray shots. Compared to its Trade Union counterpart, the Veil hauls less crates and is not as useful in contested systems that have already been secured.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

The blueprint can also be obtained from medium Pirate Dens.
