The **Snowball** is a Tier 1 projectile that can be obtained and used at Residential Stations and The Citadel snowpiles.

The item itself could only be obtained from Syndicate freighters and FOs during the Winter 2021 update.

## Trivia

* Snowballs were never meant to be obtained in their item form. However, Keth "forgot" to remove snowballs from the sales list of Syndicate freighters and FOs.
* The price of a snowball from said freighters and FOs is surprisingly expensive, costing 75k-90k credits each.
* The snowball item **will** be used up if thrown, so don't use it unless you want to throw 75k-90k out the window.
