Mandator Line

The Mandator line, designed by the Kavani Mandate, features fast and versatile ships that use their energy advantage to gain the upper hand in most PvP situations.

### Starships

Starships in this line focus on survival and support, relying heavily on subsystems for defense, offense, and control over the opponent.

* +1 subsystem and rig slot over other starships.
* Tied for most hitpoints among interceptors.
* Highest mobility and lowest DPS among fighters.

### Warships

Warships in this line don't have any particularly weak stat; they have above-average mobility, good shielding, but most importantly: Mandator warships have unmatched set versatility.

* +1 subsystem slot over other warships.
* Highest deflection power and second highest shield regen among warships.
* Third highest overall mobility among warships.

### Turret Placement

Warships have a unique design choice: most of the turrets are on top, arranged in a 'Y' formation when viewed from the front, creating a huge area above the ship where they focus fire. In theory, this would leave the bottom and sides with little coverage, but their speed and agility make up for it, since ships of the same size or larger can't outmaneuver the Mandator warships, and keeping distance or simply kiting doesn't allow faster warships ships to get close enough to take advantage of those blind spots.

## Trivia

* This is the line used by the Kavani Sovereign Navy.
* The Mandator line starships are named after tropical birds, warships, justice-related roles, and larger ships, with expansionist labels.
* The Mandator and the Constellation lines are the only combat lines to have warships with thrusters seperate from the main body.
