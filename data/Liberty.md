Liberty

*"The Liberty corvette is a sturdy and powerful warship. It is the Lycentian go-to for routine patrols, station defense, and filling out a combat fleet."*

The Liberty is a corvette-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a sturdy fleet ship due to its high shield strength and turret agility.

## Advantages

The Liberty has a sizable shield and deflection pool, allowing it to tank on the same levels as the Chevron while still having natural regeneration as a shield-based warship. It's shield capacity can be enhanced with the right defense rigs. Its high shield strength means it has a better chance of retreating into stations in PvP, since hulled ships can't enter stations.

In addition, the turret agility bonus allows it to more easily track small, fast targets.

It has fairly balanced turret placement, able to deal with threats from many angles

Overall, the Liberty can tank damage and provide fire support/point defense in a fleet. It is a decent, though unpopular, choice for early game PvE.

## Disadvantages

The Liberty is relatively slow compared to other corvettes and has very low hull. Pilots should take care to watch their shields, as once they lose them, their hull will not last long at all.

It also has poor shield regen, though this can be fixed with the right subsystem.

As a Lycentian ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a Lycentian terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
