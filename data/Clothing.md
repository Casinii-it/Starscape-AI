Clothing

## Clothing Items

![](./File:LycentianUniformPLT.png)

Lycentian Pilot's Uniforn

In Starscape, players can wear clothing for cosmetic purposes, to show allegiance to a faction, or to display their military rank. When you first join the game, you are given a basic pair of pants and a shirt. The shirts vary in color, and the player is given a choice in the color of shirt.

Clothing is worn under Armor, and when worn without armor provides no protection against attack. Clothing is unique in that it is not lost upon death, although it will need to be re-equipped. Each NPC faction offers a uniform as a standing reward, but the military factions require you to enlist in their military to purchase their clothing.

### General Clothing

This is easily available plain clothing you can pick up at any NPC marketplace. Additionally, these clothes can sometimes be found for sale on NPC Freighters.

### Faction Clothing

This clothing is purchased with loyalty points from faction terminals. For non-military factions, uniforms are available as a tier `II` faction reward. Military uniforms that correspond to a rank require the player to be that rank in order to be equipped. See Enlisting for more information on ranks.
