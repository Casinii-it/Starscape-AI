Drones

*For a guide to hunting drones, see Drone Hunting.*

![A Drone Battleship spotted in wild space.](./File:Drone_Battleship_at_planet.jpg)

*A Drone Battleship spotted in wild space.*

**Drones** are hostile NPCs that exist in all non-core systems. They attack **any** ship that isn't a drone that comes within 2,000 meters, and they retaliate against any ship that damages them or one of their structures within 5,000 meters. The farther a player is from The Citadel, the tougher and more abundant drones will become.

Drones are an interstellar threat to humanity, and are labelled as a “shoot-on-sight” target by all NPC factions.

### Spawn Locations

* Warp hubs and rings, where drone factories and fleets can be found;
* Planetary asteroid fields and wreckage fields;
* Mission sites from CoreSec and The Syndicate;
* BF, DH and SP anomalies;
* X-structures revelead by ancient beacons.

## Drone Types

### Tiers and Appearance

There are six tiers of drones: light and standand (black hull with red lights), advanced (red hull with yellow lights), elite (navy blue hull with violet lights), command (white hull with blue lights) and ancient (grey/brown hull with orange lights). The first five tiers have increasing hitpoints and DPS for each tier above. Ancient drones use diferent models and are considerably weaker than other drones.

### Hull Types

* **Turrets** are stationary, have two turrets, and do not have shields. They can only be found at factories, hideouts and "Assault the Base" missions.
* **Fighters and Interceptors** are agile and have 2 repeater spinal guns.
* **Corvettes** carry 2 pairs of turrets on the top and bottom.
* **Frigates** carry 3 sets of 3 turrets in a trine formation.
* **Destroyers** carry 4 small turrets and 1 medium turret on the top and bottom.
* **Cruisers** carry 2 small turrets and 2 medium turrets on each side and 1 large turret on the top and bottom. They can only be found in hideouts in wild space.
* **Battleships** are boss drones that have a separate hull and shield for each part of the ship. They have a chance to spawn from drone armadas. For more information, see Drone Battleship.

## Drone Loot

* **Salvage items** such as metal scraps and power cores; the different types of **drone cores** depend on the tier and level of the destroyed drone ship or structure, respectively.
* **Axnit** is dropped by advanced and elite drones, as well as drone structures.
* **Hybrid blueprints, hybrid modules, narcor and red narcor** are dropped by elite and command drones, as well as drones structures.

## Trivia

* Most drones use energy turrets, but ancient drones use kinetic turrets.
* There was a scrapped drone ship called the Drone Mothership, which spawned as a functionless structure in an unnamed anomaly during alpha testing.
* There is also a lesser known drone dreadnought, which possibly had an order in the way to destroy it.
* During the Winter 2020-2021 event, drones had a chance of dropping Snowflake Fireworks.
