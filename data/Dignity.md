Dignity

*"(Since the cruiser is not yet player obtainable, a quote is not available)."*



The Dignity is a cruiser-type ship of the Righteous ship line, known for its role in Lycentian fleets.

## Advantages

* The Dignity can fire all turrets on a broadside, and at least half vertically.
* Like all Lycentian ships, it has a tracking bonus that allows it to combat fightercraft with ease.
* As a cruiser, The Dignity almost always has a complement of fighters and corvettes to support it

## Disadvantages

* Like all cruisers, it is very slow. This means it can easily be kited without the use of a light burner.
* The turrets are placed mostly upwards on the ship leaving two large turrets at the bottom of the ship

## Trivia

* There are four large turrets on the Dignity.
* This ship isn't player obtainable.
* The legacy Lycentian Cruiser model was called the 'Valor'.
* Despite being Lycentian, the Dignity has a strong hull focus.
