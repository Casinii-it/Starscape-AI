Miners

Miners are industrial ships that come in a variety of sizes, sharing the same focus on improving mining rate, but are far worse at defending themselves from players and NPCs because they are suposed to be running miner turrets and tractors all the time.

For a ranking of mining efficiency, see Mining Speed Comparison.
