Stronghold Line

The Stronghold Line features hull-based tanks designed with high DPS. However, speed and agility are utterly sacrificed for the mentioned traits.

### Fighters

Figthers in this line have the defense and damage output to face most threats in the early-game and are powerful assets in fleet battles if they can avoid being targeted. However, the low mobility makes them some of the weakest options for 1v1s.

* Highest DPS and most hitpoints among starships.
* Lowest mobility and energy capacity among starships.

### Warships

Currently, the only warship of this line has one of the best defensive and offensive loadouts in the game, making it perfect for PvE, but in PvP it is severely held back by being too slow both to engage and to disengage from fights effectively.

* Most hitpoints and second highest DPS among warships.
* Strongest broadside in the game.
* Lowest speed and warp speed among warships.

## Trivia

* The Stronghold line ships are named after medieval war themes.
* This is the combat ship line with warships with least amount warships.
