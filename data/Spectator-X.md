ZolarKeth revealed his Spectator-X in the Public Test Server during a mass test on February 17th, 2024. Its stats are similar to its predecessor's, but unlike the normal Spectator, the Spectator-X has a Pulse PDT, along with a large damage increase, and is the only Officer Class ship. This ship has only been seen in Dev blogs and the Public Test Server.

## Advantages

As stated previously, it shares some stats with the Spectator. This means it has 10000 Shield points, Hull points, Shield Regen, and Hull Regen. It surpasses the normal Spectator in mobility, with 100 more speed and acceleration, and only 50 points less in agility. All other stats are identical.

As stated before, the Spectator-X boasts a single Pulse PDT with an unholy damage boost. Keth was able to wipe out dozens of destroyers in Gatinsir in a brief period of time using it.

## Disadvantages

Cannot be obtained by any players, as it is a staff only ship.
