Spice is a type of cargo item that players can buy and sell in other parts of the galaxy to make a credit profit.

## Variants

The 6 main spice colors are found in distinct regions of the galaxy, which can be checked using the spice color filter on the galactic map. The spice color found in a system is usually also indicated by **the color of nebulae in the background skybox**, which is useful for determining the spice available in wild systems, whose colors aren't highlighted by the filter.

| Tiers | Variants | Found at: |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Colored (1) | Blue, Green, Orange, Purple, Red, Yellow | Spice platforms and SP anomalies |
| Syndicate (2) | Silver | Spice platforms in the Syndicate |
| Gemstone (3) | Sapphire, Jade, Jasper, Amethyst, Ruby, Amber | Frontier outposts and SP anomalies in wild space |

## Spice Profitability Chart

The price for which stations and frontier outposts buy spice from players increases for spice colours on the opposite side of the map and increases with the distance of the system from the Citadel.

Note that buying a variant of spice increases its price for all players in that system and in nearby systems, and selling a variant of spice will reduce its selling price for all players in that system and in nearby systems. When selling spices, the color of the selling price indicates the average profit margin; red indicates no profit or loss, orange and yellow indicate average profits, and green indicates higher profits.

## Damaged Spices

Damaged spices can be obtained by shooting spice crates, which can be ejected by a player or found in SP anomalies, transforming them into normal loot containers from which damaged spices can be acquired. These items can only be sold at market terminals for a fixed price: normal spices at 180 credits and gemstone spices at 300 credits each.
