Drone Mothership Anomaly

**Archived:** The subject of this page has been removed from the game.


![](./File:Drone_Mothership_Anomaly.png)

The **Drone Mothership Anomaly** is an old anomaly removed from the game. It contained a large Drone ship. Due to the old anomaly naming system, this anomaly type had no specific letter combo associated with it. It was removed at some point before the anomaly revamp.

## Trivia

* The unfinished anomaly was intended to later be developed into an anomaly raid boss, until it was delayed and later removed for unknown reasons. However, there is a section in the Development Roadmap that may reference this old anomaly; the "Expanded Infantry Combat" section mentions "ground combat-centric anomalies, such as a Dreadnought raid"![](./File:Aaaa.png)
* This anomaly may have been the early form of the Drone Battleship.
