Hybrid Polaris

*"The Hybrid Polaris is a technological marvel, combining human engineering with advanced drone technology to create a remarkable fighter-interceptor."*

The Hybrid Polaris is an interceptor-class ship of the Valiance ship line. It is best suited as an agile but deadly fighter-interceptor combination, and is a tech-focused sidegrade to the Polaris.

## Advantages

* The Hybrid Polaris has increased speed and agility over the standard Polaris, and it can use a second defense rig for further pushing its exceptional maneuverability.
* The Hybrid Polaris has high damage per-projectile, making it useful for quick kills.
* It has the highest shield regeneration rate of all fighters and interceptors.

## Disadvantages

* The Hybrid Polaris sacrifices 75 shield points in exchange for a second defense slot.
* Due to requiring Vexnium and its respective blueprint to produce, it is hard to obtain and risky to use, and may prove unfit for most players.

## Trivia

* The current Hybrid Polaris is modeled by Jeff\_D.
* Before Next Gen, the Hybrid Polaris was called the Polaris-X, similar to the other 3 Hybrid ships.
