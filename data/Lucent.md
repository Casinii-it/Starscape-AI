Lucent

*"The Lucent is an upgraded variant of the Lycentian Luster fighter."*

The Lucent is a T2 fighter-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a deflection-focused fighter.

## Advantages

* The Lucent ties with the Hybrid Luminar for the highest deflection rating of all fighters and can more effectively withstand PDTs.
* It deals very high damage per-projectile, which helps it achieve quick kills.
* The lucent has 2 defense modules, which can help make up for the low durability

## Disadvantages

* The Lucent has weak overall durability, which can be exacerbated by certain modules, thus often necessitating repairer subsystems to offset this downside.
* The lack of weapon rigs limits its damage potential, as being unable to boost its damage or lengthen its volleys of fire turns problematic quickly.
* As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
