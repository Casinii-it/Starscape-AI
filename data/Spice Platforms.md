Spice Platforms

*For more information, check the Spice page.*

![](./File:SpicePlatform_0.png)

A spice platform.

Spice platforms are structures that sell colored spice to players found in warp hubs of most secure, unsecure and contested systems.

## Mechanics

To buy from spice platforms, players must interact with their cargo dock while having available cargo slots and enough credits. The color of the spice sold and purchasing values depend on the system's location relative to the colored spice regions.

## Trivia

* Spice platforms have a wrecked version found in SP anomalies.
