Tetra

*"The Tetra is a Lycentian interceptor."*

The Tetra is a T1 interceptor-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a tanky starship, with agility and durability.

## Advantages

The Tetra has a balanced rig layout allowing for a fast-fighter playstyle. It excels at roles involving combat with slower, easier-to-track ships.

## Disadvantages

The Tetra is a T1 faction interceptor and, therefore, will perform poorly against its upgraded versions. Additionally, the Tetra is the least maneuverable ship in its class and, despite being advertised as the defensive interceptor, the Parrot with its subsystems and the Zenith with its shield regeneration, both with better mobility, surpass the Tetra in its niche.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
