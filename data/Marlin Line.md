Marlin Line

The Marlin line features the frailest and least efficient miners, but also the ships with the best warp-related stats. Their engine rigs allow them to spend the least amount of time outside of warp, and their low signal range can prevent players from spotting these ships, especially with the use of lightburners.

## Trivia

* Similar to the Megalodon line, the Marlin line ships are named after fish species that live on Earth.
* Despite being a neutral line, it has striking similarities to the ship lines built by The Syndicate.
