## Shrapnel turret

## Shrapnel Turret (Small)

Shrapnel turrets are kinetic turret similar to a real-life shotgun. They are unique in that they fire 5 projectiles in a spread pattern.

It is best used for close-range engagements against warships. In large quantities, it can also produce a screen of projectiles useful for keeping away fighters.

Shrapnel turrets come in Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, and an Exotic variant. The Shrapnel turret's only Exotic variant is Dread. Dread Shrapnel turrets must be crafted from scrap, a Tier 3 Shrapnel turret, and a Dread Shrapnel blueprint, obtained from Dread Pirates and Pirate Dens.

Shrapnel does not have a Medium variant.
