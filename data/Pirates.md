Pirates

*This article is about the NPC ship type. It is not to be confused with the player activity of Piracy.*

![](./File:Screen_Shot_2021-04-28_at_12.29.51_AM.png)

*Large pirate den.*

Pirates are hostile NPCs that exist in all non-core systems. They attack any ship that isn't a pirate that comes within 2,000 meters, and they retaliate against any ship that damages them or one of their structures within 5,000 meters. The farther a player is from The Citadel, stronger pirate ships and structures will be present.

### Spawn Locations

* Warp hubs and rings, where pirate dens and fleets can be found;
* Planetary asteroid fields and wreckage fields;
* Around contested system generators;
* Mission sites from The Syndicate, TU, and MG.

## Pirate Ships

### Hull Types

Pirates use the ships from neutral ship lines (all 5 neutral combat lines, the Badger line and the Marlin line). You may find pirates flying tier 1 fighters and interceptors, corvettes, frigates and destroyers.

Small miners and turrets are only found in mission sites, always having the standard tier.

### Tiers

There are four tiers of pirate ships: standard (brownish), having 1/5 of the hitpoints that the ship has when used by a player; steeled (grey/yellowish), having half; dread (orange), having the same amount; and command (brown/red), having 2 times the amount.

## Pirate Loot

* **Salvage items**, such as standing items, metal scraps and power cores;
* **Credits**;
* **Materials**, such as korrelite, reknite, gellium and axnit;
* **Dread modules, dread blueprints, narcor and red narcor** are dropped by dread ships, command ships and pirate dens;
* **Ship blueprints from factions and warp disruptors** from pirate dens.

## Trivia

* While both drones and pirates are both hostile to the player, they also will fight each other, since drones seem to attack all humans and pirates are humans.
* Pirate ships use random mixes of both kinetic and energy weapons for all types of turrets.
* Pirate fleets were added in the Getting Around update. This update also added classes of pirate interceptors and pirate command ships.
* During the halloween event of 2020, ethereal pirates would spawn, dropping a random one-time use ship skin when destroyed.
