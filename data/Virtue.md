Virtue

*"Like many Lycentian ships, the Virtue is a blocky (and thus sturdy) warship. It finds itself taking on many different roles in the Lycentian Navy."*

The Virtue is a frigate-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a fleet escort ship due to its shielding, PDTs, and turret agility.

## Advantages

The Virtue has good shielding and deflection that can be further enhanced with defense rigs.

As a frigate, it features 4 PDT, making it a good escort and screener ship. It has balanced turret placement and can deal with threats from many angles.

Because of its high shield strength, the Virtue has a higher chance of successfully retreating into a station in PvP, since hulled ships are unable to enter stations.

## Disadvantages

The Virtue has a low hull pool and will die quickly if shields go down.

It is also rather slow and can be easily kited by faster enemies.

Overall, the Virtue is more fleet-centered/defensive, and requires a skilled and cautious player to survive unsupported PvP. Unlike the Justice, it does not have broadside capabilities.

As a Lycentian ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a Lycentian terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.

## Trivia

* The current Virtue is modeled by AVeryDandyLad.

* If two Hybrid Hardlight Shield modifications are added to this ship, it will have more shield than a basic Justice.
