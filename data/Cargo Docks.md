Cargo Docks

![](./File:CargoDockModelA.png)

One of the cargo dock models.

Cargo docks are an essential part of most station-type structures for sustaining hauling activities by functioning both as sources of and storage for cargo items.

It is possible to check the functions of each cargo dock through its tabs by pressing F within 500 meters of it.

## Variations

| Found in | Tab Functions |
| --- | --- |
| Space Station | Market tab: buys spice from players; Storage tab: gives access to CoreSec's Storage NetworkTM and to a local cargo storage (a maximum of 500 cargo per station); Mission tab: allows the player to pick up or deliver mission items |
| Faction Outposts | Mission tab: allows the player to pick up or deliver mission items |
| System Headquarters | Ressupply tab: allows the player to deliver supplies |
| Spice Platforms | Market tab: sells colored spice to players |
| Dockyard (Frontier Outposts) | Market tab: sells gemstone spice and buys spice from the player |
| Dockyard (Staging Area) | Supply tab: allows the player to pick up/buy supplies |

## Trivia

* Residential stations don't have cargo docks.
* Cargo depots work similarly to cargo docks with a storage tab, having a local storage system for inventory and cargo items.
