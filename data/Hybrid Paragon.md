Hybrid Paragon

*"The Hybrid Paragon is a technological marvel, combining human engineering with advanced drone technology to create the fastest interceptor in the known galaxy."*

The Hybrid Paragon is an interceptor-class ship of the Defiance ship line. It is best suited as the zenith of interceptor lines and a powerful scout ship, and is a tech-focused sidegrade to the Paragon.

## Advantages

* The Hybrid Paragon has the fastest potential speed of any combat ship, and can quickly jump between systems.
* Unlike the standard Paragon, the Hybrid Paragon comes with a defense rig and can either further increase its blistering speed or take up passive hull regeneration without needing a repair Subsystem.
* The Paragon is the most agile ship in the game.

## Disadvantages

* Despite having increased damage over the standard Paragon, the Hybrid Paragon still has a low damage output and is generally unsuitable for combat.
* The Hybrid Paragon has only 220 hull points compared to the standard Paragon which has 250, hurting its durability.
* Due to requiring Vexnium and its respective blueprint to produce, it is hard to obtain and risky to use, and may prove unfit for most players.

## Trivia

* The current Hybrid Paragon is modeled by swindle150.

* Before Next Gen, the Hybrid Paragon was called the Paragon-X, similar to the other 3 Hybrid ships.
* The Paragon-X was one of the most sought after interceptor ship for PVP, and would be very expensive for both the ship and its 1/2 and 2/2 Blueprints.
