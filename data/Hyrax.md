Hyrax

*"The Hyrax is an entry-level hauler. Though it lacks both in capacity and agility, it is a good step for someone looking to advance their career in cargo hauling."*

The Hyrax is an entry-level hauler-class ship of an Mastodon ship line. It is best suited as a cheap starter hauling ship.

## Advantages

The Hyrax is cheap and can be bought at most NPC marketplaces, making it the easiest ship to start hauling with. It is useful for taking on low tier hauling missions for standing, credits, or skill points. The Hyrax (and any other hauler, really) can be used in tandem with a piracy fleet in order to deliver extreme amounts of warp disruptors.

## Disadvantages

The Hyrax is an entry-level ship, and thus will perform poorly compared to upgraded haulers. It is generally inferior in cargo capacity, speed, and durability to any other fighter-style hauler, and is only useful as a cheap starter ship.
