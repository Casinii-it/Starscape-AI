Echelon

*"The Echelon is a slow and sturdy beast of a warship, capable of withstanding an incredible amount of structural damage before yielding thanks to its redundant hull plating."*

The Echelon is a destroyer-class ship of the Legionis ship line. It is best suited as an armored fleet ship.

## Advantages

The Echelon, together with the Bulwark, is the best ship for farming drones and pirates. It has the highest armor rating and energy capacity in the game, so, along with the hull repairer, the Echelon boasts the best durability in prolonged battles, making it near invulnerable to the already reduced damage from NPCs and high RoF turrets, such as blasters.

## Disadvantages

Despite its niche in PVE, the Echelon does not excel in PVP. Like the Phalanx, its turret placement is poor, and it will never being able to use full DPS, firing 3-4 turrets most of the time against any skilled player.

The Echelon has the standard Legionis warship weaknesses: kinetic weapons and kiting.
