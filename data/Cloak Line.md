Cloak Line

The Cloak Line, built by The Syndicate, features haulers with decent field stats, except for defense. Their engine rigs allow them to spend the least amount of time outside of warp, and their low signal range can prevent players from spotting these ships, especially with the use of lightburners.

## Trivia

* The Cloak line ships are named with synonyms for cloak.
* These haulers store their cargo crates under the ship, 'cloaked' by the hull.
