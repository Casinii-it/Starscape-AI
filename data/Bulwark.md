Bulwark

*"The Bulwark destroyer is a powerful, unyielding warship. It makes up for its lacking agility with immense firepower and defensive capabilities."*

The Bulwark is a destroyer-class ship of the Stronghold line; a tanky, high-damaging warship, great for PvE. However, its lack of mobility means it cannot pursue targets or escape from combat as effectively as other ships.

## Advantages

The Bulwark’s combination of high hull pool, DPS, and full broadside makes it one of the best PvE ships - Though not near invulnerable like the Echelon, it takes out drone factories and pirate dens faster. These qualities carry through to Faction Warfare, where, while not as fast as the Loxodon, it can better withstand enemy fire and will rarely have to retreat to heal.

Despite its shortcomings in PvP, it is decent against other destroyers in contested space because the Bulwark is an excellent brawler, so going head-first against one is usually not a good play, and its hull pool can buy pilots enough time to jump out while under fire.

## Disadvantages

The Bulwark has the lowest speed stat of any combat ship in the game, so lighter warships, railguns, and kiting shut it down completely. Due to its low warp-related stats and no engine rig slot, disengaging from an enemy with warp disruption is almost impossible.
