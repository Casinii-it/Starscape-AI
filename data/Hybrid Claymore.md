Hybrid Claymore

*“The Hybrid Claymore is a technological marvel, combining human engineering with advanced drone technology to create the ultimate tank of a fighter.”*

The Hybrid Claymore is a fighter-class ship of the Stronghold ship line. It is best suited as a fast but heavy fighter, and is a tech-focused sidegrade to the Claymore.

## Advantages

* The Hybrid Claymore has amazing potential versatility with modules. As it can be modified for incredible speed, agility or hull tanking capabilities.
* It has the highest damage output of all fighters.

## Disadvantages

* The Hybrid Claymore has slower shield regeneration than the standard Claymore, but it is better in every other statistic.
* Due to requiring Vexnium and its respective blueprint to produce, it is hard to obtain and risky to use, and may prove unfit for most players.

## Trivia

* The current Hybrid Claymore is modeled by Jeff\_D.
* Before Next Gen, the Hybrid Claymore was called the Claymore-X, similar to the other 3 Hybrid ships.
