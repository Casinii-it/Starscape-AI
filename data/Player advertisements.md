Player advertisements

## Overview

Player advertisements were added as a separate update, and the second of Keth's return. they allow players to show other players things, but across systems and repetitively. they can be purchased using credits or stardust.

## Function

Player advertisements were added with the intent of being a method of promoting Player Factions, market orders, or other various things. They are purchasable at most stations across Secure and Unsecure space, and all Core systems.

Player advertisements cost both credits and stardust to post. However, Robux can be used as an alternative, although this is not reccomended.

Ads have customizable parameters, such as duration, visibility, and the actual contents of the ad. The more specifications in the request, the more it costs to purchase the ad. Ad specifications are listed here:
