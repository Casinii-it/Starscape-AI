Furniture

Furniture are items that provide decoration to a player's apartment on a Residential Space Station. Most furniture items can be purchased in residential stations or on the Player Marketplace, while banners and holoprojector's are rewards from Factions.

## Furniture Items

There is a variety of furniture items available such as

* Chairs, Couches and Beds
* Shelf's, Counters and Display Cases
* Tables and Endtables
* Banners and Holoprojectors
* Plants
