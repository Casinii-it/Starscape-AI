Deadspace

Deadspace is a playable area in Starscape that cannot be visited without specific credentials. Squads bypass deadspace restrictions - anyone in a squad can warp towards other members' locations.

### By Default

- Ancient Beacons: If a player has an ancient beacon and navigates to the indicated system, a deadspace X-structure is revealed.

- Missions Sites: they are generated in a system after accepting a mission from a field office agent, and disappear once the player leaves.

### After Depletion

- Aberrations: once all secure containers at X-structures are looted and all asteroids in deposits are mined, the subregion becomes deadscape.

- Anomalies: except for WH, FO, and MT anomalies, once all available loot is collected, the area disappears from the system map after the player warps out.

## Trivia

* Even if you leave a deadspace region and it disappears from the system map, other players in that region will remain there, and you can't go back without being in their squad.
* If a player quits the game while in a deadspace region, they will be in some warp hub in the system once they join back.
