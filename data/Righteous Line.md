Righteous Line

The Righteous line, constructed by the Lycentian Federation, features ships with unparellel shielding at the cost of most of their speed and agility.

### Starships

Unfortunately, there aren't enough factors to define these ships. They have low mobility stats, while the rest are just average, being completely outclassed by the Aurora and the Parrot lines.

* Highest deflection power among starships.
* Tied for lowest speed and agility among starships.

### Warships

Warships carry over the line's defining features along with an innate turret turn speed buff to compensate for low agility, making them excellent ships for PvE and the anti-meta choice against kinetic turrets.

* Largest shield pool among warships.
* 1.75x turret turn speed.
* Lowest speed and shield regen among warships.

### Turret Placement

Medium ships have their turrets spread across all faces of the ship, which means that there are no blind spots, making them great fleet ships and the biggest counters to interceptors. The Justice is very similar to the Mandator and Stronghold line destroyers, with most of the turrets on top, but also allowing full broadside firepower and 4 PDTs to keep starships at bay.

## Trivia

* This is the line used by the Lycentian Federal Starfleet.
* The Righteous line warships are named after morality and democracy principles.
  + The starships are named after adjectives about reflecting light.

* The ships of this line, before the Next Gen update, had very blocky designs, thus earning the nickname 'bricks'.
* All warships in this line have 0.7 warp speed.
