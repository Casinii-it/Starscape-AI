Faction Standing

![](./File:FactionStandingTab.png)

Faction standing in the Career tab.

Faction standing is the measurement of a player's reputation with a faction. The current range of faction standing goes from -2000 to 5000 standing points.

## Obtaining

Faction standing is earned by completing missions for a faction and by turning in standing items; in the case of three warring factions, it also includes hauling supplies to system headquarters and capturing systems.

### Loyalty Points

![](./Loyalty_Points "Loyalty Points")Loyalty Points are an in-game currency that has a version for each of the factions, being earned when faction standing is gained at a 1:1 ratio, but they aren't lost if you lose standing with that faction.

Loyalty points are used to buy items in faction terminals and military rank promotions.

## Negative Standing

Upon attacking and destroying faction ships, standing with that faction will go down by 10 points per destroyed fighter or interceptor, 15 per corvette, 25 per frigate, and 50 per destroyer. At certain levels of negative standing, the faction will take action against the offending player.

| Tier | Tier FSP Requirements | Faction Actions |
| --- | --- | --- |
| -1 | -500 | This level of standing prevents the player from entering any of the faction’s stations. |
| -2 | -1500 | All ships and stations from that faction will attack the player on sight, even in core and secure systems. If the player is to reach this level of standing, they will receive the title 'Enemy of the Empire, Mandate, Federation, Trade Union, Syndicate, Mining Guild, or People' for Foralkus, Kavani, Lycentia, Trade Union, Syndicate, Mining Guild, and CoreSec, respectively. |

## Resetting Standing

If players have negative standing and wish to reset it, go to their respective embassies in the Citadel. There, tell the ambassador that you want to fix your standing. The ambassador will offer players a pardon only if they pay reparations, paid at a rate of **5 credits per standing point**. Once paid, the ambassador will have another dialogue, "finalizing your pardon". Players can also turn in items for standing to increase their standing manually.

**IT IS NOT POSSIBLE TO RESET YOUR STANDING WITH CORESEC WITHOUT THE USE OF STANDING ITEMS (please note, you will be unable to enter the Citadel if you have -500 standing with CoreSec).**

  + Inversely, reaching Enemy status with all factions grants you the "Enemy of the Galaxy" title. This title is a good bit rarer than Galactic Hero, as it takes a lot more effort to get, particularly because of how long it takes to find and destroy enough CoreSec transport ships, and Trade Union and Syndicate freighters.
