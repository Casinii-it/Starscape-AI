The Edge Of Space

![](./File:EdgeOSpace.png)

The Edge of Space is an invisible barrier that prevents players from leaving the playable spacebox. It is approximately 16 km from the warp-in area. Upon reaching it, all colors will fade to black and white, and a yellow banner message will appear telling you that "you are at the limit of this part of space".

Not stopping the ship once it reaches the Edge of Space will trigger pop-ups to unstuck player ships, just like ramming into other objects, such as asteroids and stations.

Players cannot go beyond the Edge of Space without traveling.
