Monuments

Monuments are circular structures with rings orbiting around a colored core. Their sole purpose is to grant blessings (i.e., buffs) to player ships. These objects appear in three variations: tier 1, tier 2, and tier 3, in blue, green, and red, respectively. Each tier grants superior buffs to the previous one.

Tier 1 and 2 monuments are displayed in the quick-warp menu ![](./File:MonumentIcon.png) and are listed as points of interest on the planet map. Level 3 monuments appear as anomalies.

## Usage

To use a monument, players must fly their ship within 500 meters of the core and press F to receive the blessing. A pop-up will appear informing that the ship has been blessed with a specific bonus. The buff the monument granted is visible in the left corner of the screen, as is the bonus's effectiveness and duration (always 30 minutes).

Players can only receive one blessing per monument per hour, but can get the blessing from as many monuments as they come across. If a monument grants the player a blessing that their ship already possesses, the effect will be replaced and the duration reset, even if the previous blessing was of a higher tier.

### Variations

**- Tier one** monuments grant T1 blessings and are found at warp hubs of non-core systems.

**- Tier two** monuments grant T2 blessings and are found at warp hubs of non-core systems and the capital systems of the warring factions.

**- Tier three** monuments grant T3 blessings and are found at MT anomalies, surrounded by a ring of 24 pristine ore asteroids. The ore type depends on the system's location in the galaxy. Systems in the Core will almost always have korrelite or reknite, and systems on the edge of wild space will usually have gellium. A monument missing some or all of its asteroid ring means another player has recently mined it out.

## Blessings

List of Known Blessings

| Blessing Name | Blessing Description | T1 | T2 | T3 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mining efficiency | Increases the number of ores produced when mining asteroids | +15% | +30% | +50% |
| Hull resistance | Increases the resistance of a ship's hull to damage | +5% | +14% | +20% |
| Drone damage | Increases damage against drones | +10% | +20% | +30% |
| Damage | Increases damage against all | +3% | +7% | +12% |
| Ship speed | Increases ship movement speed | +5% | +12% | +20% |
| Shield resistance | Increases the shield's ability to withstand damage | +5% | +10% | +20% |
| Shield recharge | Increases the rate at which the shield is recharged | +10% | +20% | +40% |
| Rate of fire | Increases fire rate | +3% | +7% | +12% |

## Trivia

* On May 17, 2020, Tier 3 monuments were changed to anomalies.
* Tier 3 monuments used to have a chance to "bless your ship with superior capabilities," giving all tier 2 bonuses at once.
* Originally, the asteroid rings at tier 3 monuments were always korrelite. This was changed on August 16, 2020, sometimes to be reknite or gellium instead.
* Monuments were remodeled on March 9, 2024, gaining a new effect for the rings, with new animation and sound when activated.
  + This update also changed the type of ore that spawned around tier 3 monuments to vary depending on distance from the core.
