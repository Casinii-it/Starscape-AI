Fightercraft

Fighters are small combat ships, differing from interceptors for usually having greater damage output and defenses, but much lower speed. Their stats are balanced enough for PvE and, in PvP, the DPS is extremely high for such cheap ships, really standing out in fleet battles.

The Stratos, despite being classified as a starter ship, is placed in this page for also being a slower combat starship.
