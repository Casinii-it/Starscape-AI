## The Starscape Map

![Map 2024](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/starscape-roblox/images/7/73/Map_2024.png/revision/latest?cb=20241028205753 "Map 2024")


Starscape's full map as of the Getting Around Update (11th March 2024)



Starscape consists of 4576 charted systems represented by colored dots, divided by regions, faction borders, and sectors. Each one includes planets orbiting their stars in real time, along with anomalies and aberrations that appear and disappear regularly.

For individual systems important to the game and its history, see Notable Systems.

## The Galactic Map

The Galactic Map can be brought up in the game by pressing the 'M' key. Maps for the 3 constested sectors also appear in HQs as physical tables.

In the top-left corner its possible to select what the player wants to be highlighted in the map by changing the color of the systems. There are 4 map filters:

- Security classes, showing which rules apply to PvE and PvP on each system.

- Spectral class, showing the 'color' of the system's main star.

- Spice color, showing what color of spice is found there in spice platforms and SP anomalies.

- Visited systems, highlighting in green systems the player visited before and making the rest grey.

## The System Map

The star system map can be accessed by pressing 'N'. It shows a map of your current system, with planets, anomalies, secondary and tertiary stars orbiting the main star. Clicking on any region or sub-region of the system reveals a full report, including its orbital period, and allows for the player to align their ship and warp to that location.
