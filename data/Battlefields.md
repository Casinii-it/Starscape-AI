Battlefields

![](./File:Battlefield.png)

Battlefields and wreckage fields consist of the wreckage of groups of ships with loot containers scattered throughout, generated in BF anomalies and warp hubs, respectively. Drones and pirates often spawn here.

### Loot Containers

The loot containers' icons will only become visible when the player gets close. They can spawn around, on, or inside wreckages. Possible loot includes low-tier weapons, armor, materials, and salvage items, such as metal scraps, power cores, and standing items.

Wreckage fields regenerate containers over time, but battlefields do not; BF anomalies will become deadspace once all loot containers are collected.

## Trivia

* Unlike regular destroyed ships, those found in battlefields and wreckage fields aren't intangible.
* The shipwrecks found include neutral fighters, small miners, and faction warships.
  + Often, battlefields and wreckage fields will contain ship models from before the *Next Gen Update*, such as the old freighters and cruisers.
* Loot container models are identical to the containers ejected by player ships.
* Wreckages of Kavani cruisers will only have weapons or armor in their containers.
* The *Salvage Operation* mission will spawn a battlefield inside a minefield, but those have mission cargo instead of item containers.
* There is a wreckage field in Foralkus to serve as a memory of their war of independence.
