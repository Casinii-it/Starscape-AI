Skills

## Overview

Added in the Next Gen Update, **skills** give players small buffs when elements of a specific set of skills -commonly referred to as skill trees- are upgraded. More skills are unlocked the further you progress along the tree.

There are currently 4 skill trees in game, which reflect the four main ventures: **Combat, Industry, Commerce**, and **Exploration**.

## Combat

Affects the damage you deal and the amount of attached ship modules you lose on death (except in wild systems).

### Ways to obtain XP

* Destroying NPC ships
* Destroying player ships
* Completing Combat missions

## Exploration

Affects the hacking of Crates and the use of Scanning Probes.

### Ways to obtain XP

* Discovering new systems\*
* Scanning Anomalies and Aberrations
* Hacking crates found in X-Aberrations
* Completing Exploration missions

### Skills:

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hacking | +5% hacking operation time allowance | None | 100 | 250 | 750 | 2500 | 10000 |
| Power Systems Knowledge | +10% chance to forgive hacking operation mistakes | Hacking level 3 | 300 | 750 | 2250 | 7500 | 30000 |
| Lockout Circumvention | -10% hacking operation lockout time | Hacking level 4 | 500 | 1250 | 3750 | 12500 | 50000 |
| Probe Operation | -5% probe launch cooldown | None | 100 | 250 | 750 | 2500 | 10000 |
| Probe Longevity | +5% system probe lifetime | Probe Operation level 3 | 400 | 1000 | 3000 | 10000 | 40000 |
| Probe Navigation | +5% system probe speed | Probe Operation level 4 | 500 | 1250 | 3750 | 12500 | 50000 |
| Probe Radius | +5% anomaly detection radius | Probe Operation level 2 | 300 | 750 | 2250 | 7500 | 30000 |

### Activity Values:

* 0 Skill Points for claiming open crates, 50 for hacking locked containers
* 15 Skill Points per scanned aberration, 25 per scanned anomaly
* 25 Skill Points per new system\*

\*This means Systems not previously visited by you; use the 'Visited systems' filter in the Galaxy Map to find undiscovered systems.

## Trivia

* 6 skill trees were planned (Piloting, Combat, Industry, Commerce, Innovation, and Civics), but half were cancelled due to development issues
  + The Civics skill tree was green, Innovation was purple, and Piloting was light blue; Piloting initially included 16 skills, unlocked the ability to drive certain classes of ships, and could boost some properties of a ship class by its respective upgraded skill
  + The color of skills may have been partly inspired by the 6 main spice/system colors
* Skill trees are officially referred to as constellations, but this is only seen in-game in the Interface tutorial
