Battleships

Battleships are massive capital ships, having the largest amount of hitpoints and turrets in Starscape, serving as the ultimate PvE challenge. Currently, they are not available to players and are only seen in use by NPCs of the Foralkan Empire, the Kavani Mandate, the Lycentian Federation, and by drones.

| Line | Mandator | Megalodon | Righteous | Drone |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Battleship | Conqueror | Ptychodus | Nobility | Battleship |

Faction battleships are found in stronghold contested systems in the planet where the HQ is located, spawning in capital shipyards, while drone battleships have a chance to appear after defeating a drone armada.

## Trivia

* Faction battleships' lack of loot combined with how hard it is to take one down, they are usually better avoided than battled.
* Battleships have a unique theme when in combat.
  + Faction battleship theme.
  + Drone battleship theme.
