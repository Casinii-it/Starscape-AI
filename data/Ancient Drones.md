Ancient Drones

Ancient Drones are unique variants of drones found in X-structures. They were added in the Echoes of the Past update on the 3rd of June, 2021.

## Overview

The wrecks of these ships can be found in regular derelict X-structures. Their intact variants, which are hostile by default, can only be found in X-structures revealed by ancient beacons. Only a corvette and a fighter variant exist for this tier, with models that are radically different from the standard drone ship models.

#### Technology

Unlike other drones, they lack shielding and are considerably slower and weaker. The corvettes use railguns and coilguns, whereas the fighters use a unique kinetic spinal gun.

### Loot

Ancient drone wrecks can only drop salvage items, such as metal scraps, power cores, and ancient drone cores.

## Trivia

* They are all named "Ancient Drone" regardless of hull type.
* When targeted by PDTs and subsystems, the text identifying the faction to which ancient drones are associated is 'Expanse'.
