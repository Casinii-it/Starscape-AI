Juno

*"The Juno is a massive mining freighter which can sustain itself on deep space mining trips for absurd lengths of time without running out of cargo space."*

The Juno is a massive miner/hauler of the Koronis ship line that draws heavy comparison to the Yukon due to similar capabilities.

## Advantages

Compared to the Yukon, it has a larger health pool and a faster base speed. It also has a medium turret slot, loads of point defense turrets and also lacks the damage penalty of most warship-sized miners. Making it much better at defending itself.

Its biggest advantage over the Yukon is the 16 cargo slots, which allows the Juno to stay in wild space for an extremely long time without the need to go to a station to store items. It also has the benefit of hauling when the situation arises. If a spice trade route is discovered whilst wild exploring, then there is no need to change the ship to effectively WH/FO spice trade.

The Juno is given the benefit of two subsystems, allowing greater survivability and versatility.

## Disadvantages

While generally serviceable as a mining ship, it's completely out-classed in this regard by the true mining ships like the Ozark, Kodiak and Yukon, and filling all 16 cargo slots with ores and other resources will slow the Juno to a crawl.

Though it allows usage of a medium turret, it can only carry 4 combat turrets at once and the underside is lightly protected, so plan accordingly to these limitations.

The Juno also has a low energy capacity for its relative size, and without a reactor module slot, the two subsystems may exhaust the ship of energy. Frugal usage of subsystems and staying time in systems are of utmost importance.

The ship has the same material costs as a Yukon, but needs a blueprint and tier 5 positive standing with the Mining Guild on top of that, thus making it harder for neutral players to craft.
