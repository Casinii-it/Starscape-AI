Minefield

![](./File:Minefield.png)

a picture of a minefield in looku planet 2

Minefields are planetary objects that you can encounter in all type of systems excluding core, and appears in planetary objects as "Nothing of interest".

They are a cluster of spacemines that explode upon being shot and being flown into.

## Trivia

* Minefields may be a great place for smaller ships to retreat from bigger ships.
* Although it can be found it almost all systems, they are much rarer in secure systems.
* Any planet that has a minefield will rarely have enemies in it.
