Galactic Highways

![](./File:WarpRelay.png)

A warp relay.

Galactic highways, or the relay network, are chains of warp relays that allow players to traverse multiple systems in a single jump. Upon entering, a 10-second countdown will begin. When it finishes, the relay will initiate a different warp animation, and players will be transported to the next system. Player ships will be enveloped in electrical particles upon entering the system, and the warp tunnel will also have this unique effect. Every warp relay has a corresponding relay on the next system that makes the opposite route.

There are 5 main relay routes connecting the systems around the Citadel to all the faction capital systems, except Syndica. The capitals of the warring factions are also connected to their two staging areas and to a system closer to wild space.

Each relay jump has a price determined by the minimum number of warp jumps required to reach the system to which the relay is connected. The price without discount is 50 credits per skipped system plus 2 credits per cargo hauled per skipped system. This fee is reduced if the player has high enough standing with the faction that controls the relay, or waived if they are enlisted in their military.
