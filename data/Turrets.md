Turrets

*For information in specific turret types, check the Category:Turrets page. For information in specific turret stats, check the Statistics page.*

Turrets are items which can be equipped by ships with S, M, or L turret slots, being the main damage source of warships, including corvettes, frigates, and destroyers. Most turrets follow the cursor and fire by pressing (or by holding then releasing) their respective fire key if the turret's crosshair is aligned with the cursor.

Upgrading combat turrets only improves their damage and upgrading miner turrets typically improves their mining rate. All fourth tier turrets (X for combat turrets and IV for mining turrets) require blueprints to be built, however third tier miner turrets require them as well.

## Trivia

* Laser color cosmetics purchased in the aesthetics shop will not affect the color of miner beams.
* Railguns and coilguns are the only turrets to have 2 types of exotic variants (dread and ancient, both with equal stats).
* It was planned to have a 'tesla' turret, which shoots lightning, built from crystal shards from Crystalline Guardians.
* Drones have different turret models: a long-barrel turret that shoots similarly to blasters and a short-barrel turrets that shoots similarly to beams.
