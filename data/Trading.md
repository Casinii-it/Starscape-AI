Trading

## Overview

Trading is a feature that allows you to trade items and/or credits with other players.

There is no kind of moderation that will punish people for making bad trades. Players can overprice their items or try to convince you to sell your items for cheap. Be smart when making trades, and try to get opinions from others before making a trade.

![an example of a good deal](./File:Good_deal.png)

## How to Trade

To trade with another player, get close enough to them or their ship for the Context Menu to appear. When it does, hold F and release F on the button labeled 'Trade.'
