Editing Menu

Player ships can be customized to have different weapons, increased stats, and special abilities. They can also be customized to have different paint jobs, insignias, laser colors, and exhaust trails.

## Menu

The ship edit menu can be accessed by going to a shipyard terminal, found in all docking bays, and selecting the ship, then selecting "Edit Loadout". Alternatively, you can edit an already-spawned ship by going over its icon and pressing "C". There is also an uneditable version of the menu can also be viewed from the Spacecraft tab in your Inventory by selecting the ship and select "View Loadout" to access it.

### Options

The customization menu has several elements:

- On the top-left corner, you can see the **cosmetic** slots in which you equip skins, symbols, lasers and trails.

- On the bottom-left corner, you can see the **rig** slots in which you equip WEP, DEF, ENG, and RCT rigs.

- On the bottom-right corner, you can see the **subsystem** slots of your ship.

- On the top-right corner, you can edit the **fighter hardpoints** (F), the front-faced weapons used by most small ships.

- In the center, you can see a 3D visual representation of your ship. This is also where you equip **turrets** (S, M) and change your **point defense turrets** (P). Turrets are equipped through items you own, while PDTs are changed through a credit fee.

### Combat statistics

On the very right sidebar, you can see a list of your ship's combat statistics. You can also toggle whether skills affect the calculations, and whether or not the DPS counter displays Damage Per Second (the combined damage per second stats of all turrets and PDTs), alpha damage (the total damage of one volley from your ship; useful for low RoF turrets, like railguns and beams) or mining rate (the average number of ores released per minute).

## Trivia

* The base DPS combat statistic of a warship uses tier 1 blasters and repeaters for its sum.
  + Each PDT hardpoint adds 12.5 DPS, each small turret slot, 42 DPS, and each medium turret slot, 96 DPS.
  + If you calculate the DPS of the Sunfish, the only ship with large turrets that we know the stats of (even if they are not final), each large turret slot also adds 96 DPS.
  + Megalodon warships' turret damage increase doesn't affect the displayed base DPS.
