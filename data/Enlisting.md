Enlisting

Players can enlist in the Federal Starfleet, the Imperial Armada, or the Sovereign Navy by interacting with NPC recruiters from their respective factions, located in all their field offices next to the faction terminal and in their Citadel embassy.

Upon enlisting, players will not be able to damage their respective faction's NPC ships, as well as other players enlisted in the same faction, in contested systems. Players cannot leave the military unless they have served for at least 24 hours. Enlisting is the only way to fully participate in faction warfare, granting access to their system headquarters and permission to resupply warfare structures and capture enemy systems.

To rank up, players need to find a recruiter and apply for a promotion after acquiring enough loyalty points.

### Resigning

Upon resigning, the player loses access to all the benefits of military ranks (military items from faction terminals, titles, etc.), but not to the faction standing rewards or the loyalty points acquired; upon re-enlisting, the player will be demoted to a rank one level below the previous one (e.g., a former Kavani centurion, upon re-enlisting, will start as a Kavani monitor), unless they were a pilot.

## Trivia

* From promotion rewards, the Kavani reward their interceptors, while the Lycentian and Foralkan reward their fighters. This is because in the early stages of Starscape's development, these were the only small ships available to each faction.
