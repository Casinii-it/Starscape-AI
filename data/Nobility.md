The Nobility is the Lycentian Battleship.

## Overview

The Nobility is a lycentian battleship that can only be found in stronghold Contested Systems with capital shipyards. it takes 30 minutes to be built once a capital shipyard is constructed.

## Advantages

* As a lycentian battleship, it is predicted to have high shields and a turret agility buff. However, it may possess a strong hull focus, as the Dignity has this instead of high shields.
* Similar to other battleships, it is very hard to destroy due to high health and damage.
