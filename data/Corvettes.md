Corvettes

Corvettes are the smallest and cheapest warships. Although they are slower than starships, these ships can deal and take much more damage, as well as gain versatility with access to small turrets. They are seen as a requirement for mid-game PvE and, for PvP, their high speed and agility in relation to the heavier warships allow for strategies similar to interceptors, as well as kiting.
