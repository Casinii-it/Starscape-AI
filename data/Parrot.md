Parrot

*"The Parrot is a Kavani interceptor based on the Falcon chassis. Though not quite as swift, this ship has a significant improvement in offense and defense from the Falcon."*

The Parrot is a T1 interceptor-class ship of the Mandators ship line. It is best suited as a tech-focused interceptor.

## Advantages

The Parrot's subsystems allows skilled pilots to punch above its weight through careful use of subsystems. As an interceptor, it is also good for outmaneuvering slower fighters or warships.

## Disadvantages

The Parrot is an T1 faction interceptor, and thus will perform poorly against its upgraded counterparts. As both a Kavani ship and an interceptor, it is fragile and if caught under fire without energy or a ready pilot, it will be pummeled quickly.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
