## Blaster Turret

## Blaster Turrets

**Blaster** turrets are well-rounded , fast-firing energy-based turrets. These turrets have an average turn rate, average accuracy, and deal average damage. They have no buffs or debuffs against certain types of health.

Blasters come in Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, and an Exotic variant. The Blaster's Exotic variant is Hybrid. Hybrid Blasters must be crafted from Drone Cores, a Tier 3 Blaster, and a Hybrid Blaster blueprint dropped by high-tier Drones and Drone Factories.

## Blaster-M Turrets

Blaster-M turrets are very similar to their S variant. They have greater firepower and lower maneuverability. **Medium turrets can only be equipped onto ships with Medium Turret Slots.**

Blaster-M turrets can be Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3, or an Exotic variant. The Blaster-M's only Exotic variant is Hybrid. Hybrid Blaster-M turrets must be crafted from Drone Cores, a Tier 3 Beam-M turret, and a Hybrid Blaster-M blueprint dropped by high-tier Drones and Drone Factories.
