> *"The Mining Guild is an industrial cooperative operating in wild space. It focuses on the recovery of bulk and scarce ores for sale on the galactic market. Due to its isolated nature, the Mining Guild utilizes a fleet of proprietary mining ships which have enhanced cargo capacities to support extended mining operations."*   
> - The Mining Guild's career tab description.

The Galactic Mining Guild, based in the inner region of Beacon, is one of the economic factions in Starscape. It stands out as the second faction with territory beyond the Core, yet it remains connected to the Citadel's galactic highway. Being the source of Tier IV miner turret blueprints, having sufficient standing with this faction is a requirement for maximizing mining efficiency.

Similar to the traveling fleets of the warring faction, Quarry deploys patrol ships in the unsecure systems connecting it to Schism.

## Politics

The Mining Guild is a cooperative, effectively owned by its workers. It has neutral relations with the 3 major factions, CoreSec, and the Syndicate; it has slightly negative relations with the Trade Union, as they are the main rival operation of the Guild.
