Anomaly-type regions and subregions are hidden places that are only revealed and accessed using probes. Anomalous regions either contain unique enviroments or isolated versions of enviroments found in warp hubs and rings.

## Types

Anomaly - System anomalies (regions).

Aberration - Planetary anomalies (subregions).
