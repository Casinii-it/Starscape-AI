Mastodon Line

The Mastodon line features the smallest haulers, but also the most accessible at the start of the game, allowing new pilots to complete hauling missions from field offices. This line does not expand into heavier ship classes, making it unviable in the later stages of the game.

## Trivia

* The Hyrax is the only ship to have more than one option of upgrade.
  + The Rhino and Bison have the cheapest crafting requirements in the game, requiring much less narcor (15 and 25, respectively).
    - Both are also the only small ships to need axnit for crafting.

* The Mastodon and Everest lines are the only ones to have warships with visible cockpits.
