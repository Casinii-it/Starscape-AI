Subjugator

*"(Since the cruiser is not yet player obtainable, a quote is not available)."*

The Subjugator is a cruiser-type ship of the Mandator ship line, known for its role in Kavani fleets.

## Advantages

* The Subjugator's large turret placement is balanced between the top and bottom of the ship which allows the AI to engage a target using all 4 large turrets at 360° if horizontal
* As a cruiser, The Subjagator almost always has a complement of fighters and corvettes to support it
* As a mandator line ship, it should have extra subsystem slots.

## Disadvantages

* Like all cruisers, it is very slow. This means it can easily be kited without the use of a light burner

## Trivia

* There are four large turrets on the Subjugator.
* This ship isn't player obtainable.
