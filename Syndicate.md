> *"The Syndicate is a clandestine, privately-held trade and military organization operating out of Wild space. Little is known by the public about its internal function, but Syndicate freighters are often seen roaming the less secure parts of the galaxy, transporting assets for the organization."*  
> - The Syndicate's career tab description.

The Syndicate, based in the central region of Wildar, is one of the economic factions in Starscape. It is notable for only having unsecure systems and a single core capital, making systems around it extremely susceptible to player piracy, in addition to possessing silver spice, exclusive to the area.

Unlike any other faction, the Syndicate is the only one with a lasting presence in wild space, having most of its assets invested in frontier outposts and freighters. Within its territory, it sells supplies to warring factions and blueprints for industrial ships designed to withstand hostile environments. In short, the Syndicate covers most market niches and fills the vacuum left by its competitors.

## Politics

Little is known about the Syndicate and its operations. They have an interest in rare spices, as evidenced by their effective monopoly on tier 2 and 3 spices, and it is implied that they operate or are associated with a black market, as evidenced by the standing items accepted by them. The Syndicate does not have an official representative office in the Citadel and is located far away from the Core, where there is little CoreSec presence, and acquiring these resources is much easier. It is also presumed that they prefer to keep their distance and remain secretive, due to their investments in lightburners (escape tools) and reflective platings (stealth tools).
