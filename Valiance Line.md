Valiance Line

The Valiance line features fast ships with high damage and impressive recovery capabilities, but they struggle more with low total HP as ships get larger. They are easily available for newer pilots for their low manufacturing costs.

### Interceptors

Starships of this line possess the damage output and defense of fighters, as well as the speed of interceptors, making them some of the most dangerous and powerful starships to face in PvP.

* Most hitpoints and highest regeneration stats among interceptors.
* Second highest DPS and overall mobility among interceptors.
* Lowest energy capacity among interceptors.

### Warships

Warships in this line are built for prolonged combat and kiting, using their speed and fast recovery to make up for being the most fragile ships of their class.

* Highest regeneration stats and lowest warp charge time among warships.
* Second highest overall mobility among warships.
* Least hitpoints and lowest energy capacity among warships.

### Turret Placement

Valiance warships all have similar turret layouts: turrets spread across all faces of the ship, leaving no blind spots, but also allowing for high broadside firepower.

## Trivia

* The Valiance interceptors are named after something at the highest point or directly upwards, and the warships are named with adjectives that describe inner strength.
