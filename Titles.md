Titles

**Titles** are unlockable cosmetics that appear above your username when another player hovers their mouse on you. They are obtained by carrying out various tasks in the game, and can be viewed and changed through the Career tab.

## Venture Titles

*See also: Skills*  
Venture titles are usually earned by making progress in each of the skill trees, and require performing a wide variety of tasks to earn. The amount of skill points actually needed to earn a title may exceed the amount listed because they need to be spent to count as progress. Earning a higher title does not replace the previous title.

* Hunter
* Miner
* Tycoon
* Explorer
* Pirate

Progress towards hunter titles can be checked from the Combat skill tree. Each destroyed ship gives 10 combat skill points. Combat missions also award skill points.

| Name | Requirement | Legacy Requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rookie Hunter | 900 combat skill points earned and spent | 10 drones destroyed |
| Hunter | 4,200 combat skill points earned and spent | 50 drones destroyed |
| Adept Hunter | 20,700 combat skill points earned and spent | 250 drones destroyed |
| Veteran Hunter | 103,400 combat skill points earned and spent | 1,000 drones destroyed |
| Master Hunter | 516,800 combat skill points earned and spent | 5,000 drones destroyed |

Progress towards miner titles can be checked from the Industry skill tree. Industry skill points are given for mining asteroids, refining materials, and manufacturing equipment.

| Name | Requirement | Legacy Requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rookie Miner | 1,200 industry skill points earned and spent | 20 ore collected |
| Miner | 5,600 industry skill points earned and spent | 100 ore collected |
| Adept Miner | 27,800 industry skill points earned and spent | 500 ore collected |
| Veteran Miner | 138,800 industry skill points earned and spent | 2,000 ore collected |
| Master Miner | 693,600 industry skill points earned and spent | 10,000 ore collected |

Progress towards tycoon titles can be checked from the Commerce skill tree. Earn commerce skill points by selling items and spice or completing hauling missions.

| Name | Requirement | Legacy Requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rookie Tycoon | 600 commerce skill points earned and spent | 500 credits worth of items crafted |
| Tycoon | 2,900 commerce skill points earned and spent | 2,000 credits worth of items crafted |
| Adept Tycoon | 14,200 commerce skill points earned and spent | 10,000 credits worth of items crafted |
| Veteran Tycoon | 70,800 commerce skill points earned and spent | 40,000 credits worth of items crafted |
| Master Tycoon | 353,600 commerce skill points earned and spent | 250,000 credits worth of items crafted |

Progress towards explorer titles can be checked from the Exploration skill tree. Earn exploration skill points by visiting systems for the first time, scanning anomalies and aberrations, and hacking locked crates at X-Structures.

| Name | Requirement | Legacy Requirement |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rookie Explorer | 500 exploration skill points earned and spent | 10 systems explored |
| Explorer | 2,400 exploration skill points earned and spent | 50 systems explored |
| Adept Explorer | 12,000 exploration skill points earned and spent | 250 systems explored |
| Veteran Explorer | 59,900 exploration skill points earned and spent | 1,000 systems explored |
| Master Explorer | 299,200 exploration skill points earned and spent | 4,587 systems explored |

Unlike the other venture titles, the requirements for the pirate titles have not been changed to correspond with a skill tree. There is no longer any UI indication of their existence or progress towards the next title, though the number of player ships destroyed can be checked in the Stats section of the Career tab.

| Name | Requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Rookie Pirate | 1 player ship destroyed |
| Pirate | 5 player ships destroyed |
| Adept Pirate | 25 player ships destroyed |
| Veteran Pirate | 100 player ships destroyed |
| Master Pirate | 500 player ships destroyed |
