Yuma

*"The Yuma is a large vendor which has the capacity to place up to ten items on sale in its onboard store."*

The Yuma is a vendor-class ship of the Fargo line that can sell up to 10 items at a time in the same way that Trade Union and Syndicate ships sell items in space.

## Advantages

The Yuma provides a unique way to sell items to other players. Selling items from a Yuma offers higher visibility than placing an item on the market while also being less hassle than a direct trade. If the marketplace is taken offline, vendors become the best option to sell items.

The Yuma is the best of the two vendors, with 2 extra display screens and 6 extra item slots compared to the Fargo.

## Disadvantages

The Yuma has no weaponry of any kind, leaving it completely unable to defend itself. It's also very slow, with poor acceleration, low top speed, and slow warp speed.

## Trivia

* The crafting recipe was removed in the Next Gen Update, but was later added back Version 11.2.0, along with the Fargo.
* Because it has no weapons whatsoever, its weapon rig slot is completely useless.
* The current Yuma is modeled by Jeff\_D.
* Ever since all trading was disabled to counter a dupe glitch (Sep-Oct 2025), this ship (along with it's T1 variant) will not allow players to purchase items from it.
