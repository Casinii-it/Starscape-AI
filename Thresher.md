Thresher

*"The Thresher is an upgraded variant of the Foralkan Piranha."*

The Thresher is a T2 fighter-class ship of the Megalodon ship line. It is best suited as a heavy and aggressive fighter.

## Advantages

With proper use of its weapon rigs the Thresher can deal damage comparable to warships. It excels at a burst role, with other ships supporting it so it does not get overwhelmed or outmaneuvered.

## Disadvantages

Due to its lack of defense rigs, its durability cannot be improved. It serves as somewhat of a glass cannon - while it can deal large amounts of damage, lack of agility makes it vulnerable to being shot down.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
