Triazo

*"The Triazo is an upgraded variant of the Lycentian Tetra."*

The Triazo is a T2 interceptor-class ship of the Righteous ship line. It is best suited as a tanky fightercraft, with agility and durability.

## Advantages

The Triazo has a balanced rig layout allowing for a fast-fighter playstyle. It excels at roles involving combat with slower, easier-to-track ships.

## Disadvantages

The Triazo is the least maneuverable ship in its class and, despite being advertised as the defensive interceptor, the Macaw with its subsystems and the Polaris with its shield regeneration, both with better mobility, surpass the Triazo in its niche.

As a faction ship, its blueprint must be purchased using Loyalty Points at a faction terminal or from other players, making it less accessible to new or neutral players.
